# [TS_UI_API_LOADERS_GL_3D_TILES]

`@loaders.gl/3d-tiles` is the OGC 3D Tiles / Cesium tile-format decoder the `ui/viewer/geo` plane registers INTO `@loaders.gl/core` so `Tile3DLayer` resolves its `.b3dm`/`.pnts`/`.i3dm`/`.cmpt` tile payloads and root `tileset.json` without hand-parsing a byte. It ships a decoder FAMILY discriminated on tile content and transport behind one `Tiles3DLoaderOptions['3d-tiles']` bag, never a per-format entry vocabulary: `Tiles3DLoader` decodes the four binary tile magics and the tileset manifest, `CesiumIonLoader` is the Ion-authed variant that `preload`s an asset token then decodes the same content, `Tile3DSubtreeLoader` reads implicit-tiling `.subtree` availability bitstreams, and `Tiles3DArchiveFileLoader` unpacks a `.3tz` archive to its member `ArrayBuffer`. One `parse` returns a `Tiles3DTilesetJSONPostprocessed` for a manifest or a `Tiles3DTileContent` for a tile — batched content (a Batched/Instanced 3D Model) carries an embedded `GLTFPostprocessed` under `loadGLTF`, point content (`pnts`) carries columnar positions/colors, and `Tile3DFeatureTable`/`Tile3DBatchTable` read the per-feature metadata tables. Runtime tileset TRAVERSAL — `Tileset3D`/`Tile3D` LOD, screen-space-error, tile lifecycle — lives in `@loaders.gl/tiles`, deck-owned transitive substrate `Tile3DLayer` drives; this package owns ONLY the content decoder and the `Tiles3DLoader` registration deck's layer defaults to, worker-backed by construction through core's `parse`/`load` engine. `scope:viewer` project-local: admitted only by the `ui/viewer` Nx project, compile-time excluded from the `ui` core.

## [02]-[DECODER_FAMILY]

[TYPE_SCOPE]: the 3D-tiles decoder descriptors — one `Loader` value each, registered into `@loaders.gl/core` or passed per `parse`/`load` call. Content magic (`cmpt`/`pnts`/`b3dm`/`i3dm`), transport (open href vs Ion token vs `.3tz` archive vs `.subtree` bitstream), and manifest-vs-tile shape are the discriminants; `Tiles3DLoaderOptions['3d-tiles'].isTileset` (`'auto'`) sniffs manifest from tile, never a `parseTileset`/`parseTile` entry split.
- each content descriptor shares the tile format identity — `id: '3d-tiles'`, `name: '3D Tiles'`, `extensions: ['cmpt','pnts','b3dm','i3dm']`, `mimeTypes: ['application/octet-stream']`, magic `tests: ['cmpt','pnts','b3dm','i3dm']`, `binary: true` — so content-sniff selection through `selectLoader` resolves the decoder from the tile's four-byte magic.
- `Tiles3DLoader.parse` returns `Tiles3DTilesetJSONPostprocessed | Tiles3DTileContent` and `batchType` is `never` — 3D-tiles decodes per-tile whole; streaming a large tileset is the `Tileset3D` traversal fetch fanout in `@loaders.gl/tiles`, not a `parseInBatches` over this decoder.

Each loader's `parse` accepts `(data, options?, context?)`; the row `[SIGNATURE]` names the return and the discriminant.

## [03]-[OPTIONS_AND_TYPES]

[TYPE_SCOPE]: the `'3d-tiles'` sub-options bag threaded through `Tiles3DLoaderOptions`, the `TILE3D_TYPE` content vocabulary, and the decoded `Tiles3DTileContent`/`Tiles3DTilesetJSONPostprocessed`/`Tile3DBoundingVolume` shapes the loaders return — the vocabulary `Tile3DLayer` traversal and mesh graft consume.
- `Tiles3DLoaderOptions` — `StrictLoaderOptions & DracoLoaderOptions & ImageLoaderOptions & { '3d-tiles'?: {...} }`; the base bag (`worker`, `fetch`, `CDN`) lives in `.api/loaders.gl-core.md`, and the Draco/image sub-bags decode compressed geometry and embedded textures.
- `'3d-tiles'.loadGLTF` — parse embedded glTF binaries in-loader (default true) versus extracting `gltfArrayBuffer`/`gltfByteOffset` for an independent glTF pass; false hands the batched-model bytes to the app's own glTF decoder.
- `'3d-tiles'.decodeQuantizedPositions` — CPU-decode quantized positions when the renderer declines quantized attribute input (default false).
- `'3d-tiles'.isTileset` — `boolean | 'auto'`; the manifest-vs-tile sniff (default `'auto'`) picking `Tiles3DTilesetJSONPostprocessed` vs `Tiles3DTileContent`, never a per-shape parse entry.
- `'3d-tiles'.assetGltfUpAxis` — `'x' | 'y' | 'z' | null`; the up-axis convention applied to embedded glTF, reconciling the tile's Z-up frame with the model's Y-up.
- `TILE3D_TYPE` — `{ COMPOSITE:'cmpt', POINT_CLOUD:'pnts', BATCHED_3D_MODEL:'b3dm', INSTANCED_3D_MODEL:'i3dm', GEOMETRY:'geom', VECTOR:'vect', GLTF:'glTF' }`; the content-type discriminant on a decoded `Tiles3DTileContent.type`, driving the mesh-vs-point-vs-instance render branch.
- `Tiles3DTileContent` — `{ shape:'tile3d', type?, magic?, version?, byteLength?, header?, featureTableJson?, featureTableBinary?, batchTableJson?, batchTableBinary?, rtcCenter?, gltf?, gltfArrayBuffer?, gltfUpAxis?, tiles? (composite), featuresLength? (points/instances) }`; the decoded per-tile payload — composite tiles carry nested `tiles`, batched/instanced carry `gltf`, point tiles carry columnar attributes.
- `Tiles3DTilesetJSONPostprocessed` — `{ url, type: TILESET_TYPE.TILES3D, lodMetricValue, root: Tiles3DTileJSONPostprocessed, geometricError, asset }`; the decoded manifest with the root tile pre-resolved and content URLs absolutized for the traversal engine.
- `Tiles3DTileJSONPostprocessed` — `{ boundingVolume, geometricError, refine?, transform?, content?, contentUrl?, children, lodMetricValue?, implicitTiling? }`; one traversal node with its content href and refinement mode resolved.
- `Tile3DBoundingVolume` — `{ box?: number[12], sphere?: number[4], region?: number[6] }`; the tile spatial bound — an oriented box, a sphere, or an EPSG:4979 `[west,south,east,north,minHeight,maxHeight]` radian/metre region — driving the culling frustum test.
- `B3DMContent` — `{ batchTableJson?, featureTableJson?, featureTableBinary?, gltf?: GLTFPostprocessed, gltfUpAxis, rtcCenter: [number,number,number], type }`; the specialized batched-3D-model shape a `b3dm` tile decodes to.

## [04]-[IMPLEMENTATION_LAW]

[REGISTRY_TOPOLOGY]:
- register INTO core, app-scoped: `registerLoaders([LASLoader, Tiles3DLoader])` on `@loaders.gl/core` mutates the process-wide roster — `viewer` registers at scene graft inside the app's own Effect `Scope` and `_unregisterLoaders` at release, so two apps composing the library never leak loaders. Passing `Tiles3DLoader` explicitly per `Tile3DLayer` `loaders` prop is the collision-free alternative where an app declines the shared roster.
- transport discriminant, not a knob split: `Tiles3DLoader` decodes an open-href tileset/tile, `CesiumIonLoader` `preload`s the Ion asset token first, `Tiles3DArchiveFileLoader` unpacks a `.3tz` archive member, and `Tile3DSubtreeLoader` reads implicit-tiling availability. Select the loader by the transport — never fork a `parseIonTile` entry point onto the base decoder.
- worker-backed by default: binary tile decode runs in the core worker pool (`options.worker`); embedded glTF, Draco geometry, and textures decode through the transitive `@loaders.gl/gltf`/`@loaders.gl/draco`/`@loaders.gl/images` decoders, so no tile parse blocks the main thread.

[INTEGRATION_LAW]:
- Stack under `@loaders.gl/core` (`.api/loaders.gl-core.md`): core owns `registerLoaders`/`selectLoader`/`parse`/`load`; this package is the 3D-tiles decoder those verbs resolve. `load(url, Tiles3DLoader)` fetches-then-decodes a `tileset.json` or tile href, and `selectLoader` sniffs the four-byte tile magic — no 3D-tiles decode vocabulary is added on top of core's polymorphic pair.
- Stack under `@deck.gl/geo-layers` `Tile3DLayer` (`.api/deck.gl-geo-layers.md`): `Tile3DLayer` defaults its `loaders`/`loader` to `Tiles3DLoader` and drives the `@loaders.gl/tiles` `Tileset3D`/`Tile3D` traversal — LOD selection by screen-space error, tile fetch fanout, and lifecycle — decoding each tile's content through this loader; batched/instanced tile `gltf` renders through `scene#INSTANCED_ROWS`, point tiles feed the columnar point path. Traversal state stays deck-owned transitive; `viewer` imports ONLY this decoder registration.
- Stack with `viewer/geo#LAYER_ROWS`: this admission is the decoder the `Tile3DLayer` payload row already presumes — registering beside `@loaders.gl/las` into the one core roster, closing the "loader the layer row already presumes" gap the `ui/IDEAS#POINT_CLOUD` card opens; the `pnts` point-content path complements the `@loaders.gl/las` scan decoder on the same `PointCloudLayer` binary attribute seam.
- Stack with the tile metadata tables: `Tile3DFeatureTable`/`Tile3DBatchTable` read the decoded `featureTableBinary`/`batchTableJson` — the per-feature attributes and picked-feature metadata a `Tile3DLayer` `onClick` resolves against, never a hand-parsed table.

[LOCAL_ADMISSION]:
- imported only inside the `ui/viewer` Nx project (`scope:viewer`); the `ui` core never resolves it — heavy tile-decoder/worker deps stay compile-time excluded from non-spatial apps.
- core's loader registry is the one mutable surface: register app-scoped, `_unregisterLoaders` at release, or pass `Tiles3DLoader` per `Tile3DLayer` `loaders` prop — never assume a global roster two apps share.
- pick the decoder by transport and content: `Tiles3DLoader` for open hrefs, `CesiumIonLoader` for Ion tokens, `Tiles3DArchiveFileLoader` for `.3tz` archives, `Tile3DSubtreeLoader` for implicit-tiling availability — `isTileset: 'auto'` sniffs manifest from tile, never a per-shape parse family.
- `@loaders.gl/tiles` owns the `Tileset3D`/`Tile3D` traversal engine, reached through `Tile3DLayer`, never a direct import; the ESRI I3S variant is `@loaders.gl/i3s`, its own admission.

[RAIL_LAW]:
- Package: `@loaders.gl/3d-tiles`
- Owns: the 3D-tiles/Cesium tile decoder family — `Tiles3DLoader` (the `cmpt`/`pnts`/`b3dm`/`i3dm` and `tileset.json` decoder deck defaults to), `CesiumIonLoader` (Ion-authed `preload`+decode), `Tile3DSubtreeLoader` (implicit-tiling availability), `Tiles3DArchiveFileLoader`/`Tiles3DArchive` (`.3tz` archive), `Tile3DWriter` (inverse encode), `Tile3DFeatureTable`/`Tile3DBatchTable` (metadata tables), the `TILE3D_TYPE` content vocabulary, the `Tiles3DLoaderOptions['3d-tiles']` knob (`loadGLTF`/`decodeQuantizedPositions`/`isTileset`/`assetGltfUpAxis`), and the `Tiles3DTileContent`/`Tiles3DTilesetJSONPostprocessed`/`Tile3DBoundingVolume` output shapes
- Accept: `registerLoaders([Tiles3DLoader])` into `@loaders.gl/core` inside an Effect `Scope` with `_unregisterLoaders` at release, decoder selection by transport and `isTileset` sniff, `Tile3DLayer` driving the `@loaders.gl/tiles` traversal over this decoder, embedded glTF/Draco/texture decode delegated to the transitive decoders, `Tile3DFeatureTable`/`Tile3DBatchTable` resolving picked-feature metadata
- Reject: hand-parsing `b3dm`/`pnts` tile bytes off the worker pool, a `parseTileset`/`parseIonTile` operation family instead of registering into core's polymorphic `parse`/`load`, importing the `@loaders.gl/tiles` traversal engine directly instead of reaching it through `Tile3DLayer`, copying core's registry/decode surface into this overlay, a global loader roster two apps contend over
