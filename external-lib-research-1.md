## Monorepo design for your materials domain

The best shape for this repository is a **layered domain model**, not a flat ŌĆ£materials folder.ŌĆØ The first layer should be your **canonical taxonomy**: material families, products, assemblies, connectors, profiles, bonds/patterns, standards references, units, and uncertainty models. The second layer should be a **catalog layer** that you own. ## Further consideration

One underappreciated move would be to make your internal canonical format **analytics-native from day one**, not merely object-native. Ara3DŌĆÖs `BimOpenSchema` is notable here because it frames BIM data for **Parquet and DuckDB** workflows.
