# [INTERCHANGE_IDEAS]

The forward pool of higher-order concepts for the wire boundary. Each idea is a card — a bracketed slug leader, the capability, what it unlocks, and the gap or modern technique it draws on. An idea drives one or more `TASKLOG.md` tasks; a finished or dropped idea moves to `[2]-[CLOSED]` with a one-line disposition. The trickle-down lands here too: `DESCRIPTOR_EVOLUTION_GATE` realizes the descriptor-diff leg of the `libs/.planning` `ONE_MODEL_THREE_FACES`/`ONE_WIRE_SCHEMA_SOURCE` concert (`@bufbuild/buf` the cross-`libs/` FileDescriptorSet owner); `RECORDED_INTENT_PATCH` realizes the partial-update leg of `ONE_HEALTH_DEGRADATION_WIRE`/`CRDT_COLLABORATION` (the live-wire write-back and op-log delta cross as recorded intent); `GEO_WIRE_VOCABULARY` realizes the embedded-geometry leg of `ONE_TESSELLATION_RAIL` decoded as a typed GeoJSON owner. OPEN contains `ACTIVE` work and `QUEUED` next-up work in logical sequence; `BLOCKED` keeps open but non-actionable work; `CLOSED` separates finished `COMPLETE` items from unimplemented `DROPPED` items.
