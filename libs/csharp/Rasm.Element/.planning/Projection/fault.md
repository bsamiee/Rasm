# [ELEMENT_FAULTS]

The seam fault rail: `ElementFault` a closed `[Union]` band (band 2500) for the structural-graph, value-admission, projection, and content-verification failures every `Rasm.Element` entrypoint produces — `NodeAbsent` (an edge endpoint, a `Bake` root, or a replayed `GraphDelta` reference naming a `NodeId` the graph never declares), `RelationshipInvalid` (a `Relationship` whose endpoint node-kinds violate the seam's structural edge law — a `Compose` whose part is a non-`Object`, an `Assign` whose target is not a `PropertySet`/`QuantitySet`/`Assessment` bag, an `Associate` whose resource is not a `Material`/`Appearance`/`Coverage` — or a cyclic `Compose` ancestry `Bake` walks), `DeltaConflict` (a `GraphMutation` that puts an already-present `NodeId`, drops an absent one, or links a duplicate edge), `ValueRejected` (the band-wide rail every typed-value smart-constructor across the seam value vocabulary fails onto — a non-finite `MeasureValue` magnitude or an unresolvable unit, a non-positive `MaterialComposition` layer thickness or an out-of-unit constituent fraction, an out-of-range `MaterialPropertySet` datum or an unknown `MaterialPropertySet` vocabulary token, a band-arity or out-of-unit `Acoustic` fold input, a degenerate `CoverageGrid` or unknown `RasterSampleType`, an unresolvable `GeoReference` CRS name, or an out-of-grid `CardinalPoint` profile reference), `ProjectionFailed` (an `IElementProjection.Project` or the `Assemble` fold returning a delta the structural law rejects, or a captured projector exception lowered at the boundary), and `AddressUnstable` (the `Projection/address#CONTENT_ADDRESS` `ContentAddress.Verify` re-hash dual finding a persisted non-rooted node whose recomputed content id no longer equals its stored `NodeId` — the H7 tamper/corruption gate). The band is `Expected`-derived (`IValidationError<ElementFault>`) so band 2500 IS the `Expected` `Code` and a bare typed case lifts DIRECTLY onto the `Fin<T>`/`Validation<Error,T>` rail through the LanguageExt implicit `Error` conversion — no `.ToError()` lowering hop — each case carrying the kernel `Op` operation context and a `Category` telemetry projection, and a recovery reads `error.IsType<ElementFault.RelationshipInvalidCase>()` or `error.HasCode(2500)` rather than a message substring. The legacy per-case-code + manual `Code`/`Message`/`ToError()` shape (and the `base(detail, 2500, None)` ctor that targets the OTHER `LanguageExt.Common.Expected`) is the defect this owner closes: band 2500 is a `Code => 2500` override over the parameterless kernel `Rasm.Domain.Expected`, and the typed case IS the `Error`.

The band is one of several on the in-process `Fin<T>` federation and never re-cases its neighbors. The kernel `Rasm.Geometry/Numerics/faults#FAULT_BAND` `GeometryFault` (band 2400) carries the geometry-algorithm failures lowered through its OWN plain-union `.ToError()` (it is not `Expected`-derived); the `Rasm.Materials` bands carry the construction, connection, appearance, and material-projection failures — `Profiles/profile#PROFILE_OWNER` `ProfileFault` (2300), `Construction/assembly#PLACEMENT_MODEL` `ConstructionFault` (2350), `Connection/connection#CONNECTION_OWNER` `ConnectionFault` (2360), `Appearance/bsdf#SHADING_FRAME` `MaterialFault` (2450), and `Projection/material#MATERIAL_PROJECTOR` `ProjectionFault` (2470); the `Rasm.Bim` `Model/faults#FAULT_BAND` `BimFault` (2600) carries the BIM-and-exchange failures. So a cross-folder `Fin<T>` reads its origin from the band code, and the structural-vs-semantic split is load-bearing: the seam's STRUCTURAL edge law (endpoint-kind legality, `NodeId` resolution) is the seam's total `Switch` → `ElementFault`, while the IFC-SEMANTIC legality (containment-relating-must-be-spatial, `Void` element→opening, a `Type` may not aggregate an `Occurrence`) is the consumer's `Projection/projection#GRAPH_CONSTRAINT` `IGraphConstraint.Validate` returning `Validation<Error,Unit>` (→ `BimFault.ModelRejected` accumulated) [M3] — never a sixth seam arm, because the seam's `Switch` carries no IFC vocabulary. The content-address MINT projection is TOTAL by design (the `Projection/address#CANONICAL_WRITER` `CanonicalWriter` canonicalizes `-0.0`/`NaN`/±∞ and quantizes to `Header.Tolerance` so identity never forks), so `ContentAddress.Of`/`OfGraph` mint no fault and a non-finite scalar is rejected at its VALUE-ADMISSION gate as `ValueRejected` before it can reach the byte projection; the `ContentAddress.Verify` re-hash DUAL is the one address operation that is NOT total — a recomputed content id that no longer equals the stored `NodeId` rails `AddressUnstable` (the H7 tamper/corruption gate), so `AddressUnstable`'s sole producer is a verification mismatch, never the byte projection and never a non-finite value.

Wire posture: HOST-NEUTRAL. `ElementFault` rides the `Fin<T>` rail every seam entrypoint returns and never sits between wire and rail — the `Graph/element#ELEMENT_GRAPH` `ElementGraph.Apply` (the validated delta-replay) and `Bake` fold, the authoring `Graph/delta#GRAPH_DELTA` `WorkingGraph.Apply`, the `Properties/quantity#MEASURE_VALUE` `MeasureValue.Of`/`OfCount`/`Sum` (the SI-native `OfSi` is TOTAL — `MeasureValue`, not `Fin` — so it carries no rail), the `Properties/property#PROPERTY_VALUE` `PropertyValue.Of`, the `Composition/acoustic#ACOUSTIC_FOLDS` `Acoustic.Of`, the `Composition/material#MATERIAL_COMPOSITION` `MaterialComposition.OfLayerSet`/`OfConstituentSet` and `Composition/material#MATERIAL_PROPERTY` `MaterialPropertySet.OfMechanical`/`OfOrthotropic`/`OfThermal`/`OfEnvironmental`/`OfCost` (the discipline vocabulary `Parse` factories — `FireRating`/`Currency`/`MeasurementBasis`/`ImpactCategory` — railing the unknown-token miss), the `Geospatial/reference#GEO_REFERENCE` `GeoReference.Admit`, the `Geospatial/coverage#COVERAGE_NODE` `CoverageGrid.Of`/`RasterSampleType.Parse`, the `Relations/relation#EDGE_ALGEBRA` `CardinalPoint.Of`, the `Projection/projection#PROJECTION_CONTRACT` `Assemble` fold, and the `Projection/address#CONTENT_ADDRESS` `ContentAddress.Verify` re-hash gate (`Fin<Unit>` single-node, `Validation<Error,Unit>` snapshot sweep). The TOTAL surfaces carry no fault rail and never mint a case: `Projection/address#CONTENT_ADDRESS` `ContentAddress.Of`/`OfGraph` and `Graph/element#NODE_MODEL` `Node.ToCanonicalBytes` are total (canonicalizing rather than faulting), and `Graph/element#ELEMENT_GRAPH` `ElementGraph.Topology`/the `Element` read accessors are total. The seam carries no fault-band string-comparer accessor: `ElementFault` dispatches through its generated total `Switch`, keys no `FrozenDictionary`, and a `[KeyMemberComparer]` on the fault is the deleted form.
