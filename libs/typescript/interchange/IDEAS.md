The forward pool of higher-order concepts for the wire boundary. Each idea is a card — a bracketed slug leader, the capability, what it unlocks, and the gap or modern technique it draws on. An idea drives one or more `TASKLOG.md` tasks; a finished or dropped idea moves to `[2]-[CLOSED]` with a one-line disposition.
