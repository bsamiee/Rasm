# [CAMPAIGN_METHOD]

One planning loop drives the three peer branches — `libs/csharp`, `libs/python`, `libs/typescript` — to a decision-complete, implementation-ready bar: one method, three language doctrines, aligned never coupled, run through workflows.

Topology is `libs/.planning/ARCHITECTURE.md`; the authoring standard `libs/.planning/README.md`; form `docs/standards/`; the per-language code doctrine `docs/stacks/<lang>/`.

## [01]-[MODES_AND_CAMPAIGN_SCOPE]

PLANNING does ALL discovery — research, package mining, ideation, gap-hunt — and authors a decision-complete design corpus in which every owner is a transcription-complete CODE FENCE: the real declaration with its members, cases, and bodies in exact decompile-verified spellings, copied verbatim by an implementer.

Operating surface is the `.planning/` design corpus and its per-tier index docs including the `RULINGS.md` decision registry, the central manifests, the per-folder `.api/` catalogues, and the Nix toolchain; a source file or source tree never lands.

To "implement" or "realize" is to author or deepen a code FENCE — the markdown fenced block inside a design page that IS the work product — with the manifest, `.api`, and tooling work realization requires. A planning item's measure is the diff to its fence, never the prose framing it.

A task or idea targets a design page, a fence, a package or tooling admission, or an architecture/doc refinement; one naming source-file creation is out of scope and removed. Implementation-readiness is one test: once plan mode exits and writing begins, workflow agents need ZERO external lookups — the work and its integration points already sit on the page or its `.api` catalogue.

## [02]-[THE_BAR]

Every planning target clears the lib-grade foundation bar, never app scaffolding:
- Each library internalizes heavy logic, boilerplate, host quirks, native lifetimes, and failure handling, so apps compose it, never re-derive.
- New functionality lands as a row, case, column, policy, or receipt inside the owning fence before a new surface appears.
- Every surface is higher-order capability built FROM the admitted packages.
- Every run-placement axis carries its full host/process/deployment modality set from the root, so apps load and offload without new surfaces.
- Single entrypoints and unified rails absorb complexity internally, never by limiting tooling or hiding capability.
- Every fence holds to the route-owned code doctrine (`docs/stacks/<lang>/`) as the entry bar, graded there and never restated here.

## [03]-[WORKFLOW_ORCHESTRATION]

Scope selects the workflow shape: a full `libs/` or whole-branch target runs the multi-tiered workflow; a folder-scoped target runs the scoped workflow.

Every substantive pass runs as workflows, never one-off agents, fanned out across three altitudes and refined at each. Four adversarial stages carry it — DISCOVERY grounds the pass, CRITIQUE and RED-TEAM attack the work, and VERIFY proves and improves it to the root.

DISCOVERY grounds every downstream stage as read-only reconnaissance — read-only its one concession. It full-reads every targeted page and the folder at large, enumerates both `.api` tiers and the doctrine inventory from a real source-of-truth listing, and resolves scope against real disk state. Its product is a map, never a verdict: per-page composed capability, underutilized capability with concrete members, contextual seams, stacking guidance, and a hostile weak/strong call — carrying verified members only, never a phantom or a guess. Downstream stages re-read and exceed the map, and a map never licenses a downstream skim.

CRITIQUE is the mechanical line-by-line doctrinal-conformance and capability-completeness audit, repaired in place. It runs the named checklists — collapse scan, owner choice, knob test, aspects, rails, language modernity, and capability-plus-illusion — as a FLOOR and hunts past them; every hit is a fix, never a note.

RED-TEAM is the terminal and most aggressive review, a pre-mortem that rebuilds rather than annotates. Its move set runs a counterfactual on the core owner, algebra, or dispatch; a diff-of-the-next-feature; long-tail and failure-mode attack; boundary and strata integrity; surface-sprawl and phantom-member hunt; domain completeness; and a full cold re-review of every conformance dimension. It re-derives every composition decision — each import and admitted-package choice re-checked so a lower-stratum owner or an overlooked `.api` capability elevates the design or deletes hand-rolling — and collapses consumer overhead by internalizing orchestration behind the entry point, making policy resolution, routing, and lifecycle automatic rather than pushed onto callers. Every owner is pressured against growth, its parameterization, downstream consumers, and entry-point shape judged for the growth-demand future, with denser bigger-pass rebuilds taken wherever the opportunity exists. Counterfactual removal strips the design's central assumption — the chosen owner kind, the hand-enumerated space, the call-site dispatch, the hand-rolled kernel — derives the form the work takes without it, and builds the stronger form in place, the current shape also working never a refutation. Diff-of-the-next-feature proves the next case, dimension, or modality lands as one row with every consumer untouched or loudly broken at type-check. Every defect is repaired in place, and the work ends objectively denser and more capable than critique left it.

VERIFY is adversarial and WRITING, never a friendly confirmation. It re-derives whether the claimed work was necessary, proves on disk that it was done properly, then improves the solution to the ROOT — a single-point patch is itself the defect verify repairs wherever a root-level dense reconstruction of the same files is available. Claims stay open only when genuinely unreachable from the files at hand, never to punt a strengthenable fix.

- Tiers: per-folder, then a per-language pass, then the cross-`libs/` master.
- IDEATE runs top-down: a master concept seeds language ideas that seed folder tasks.
- IMPLEMENT and REFINE run bottom-up: each higher tier works a settled lower one.
- Each higher tier is a full pass, never a thin reconcile or aggregation: it runs the folder tier's full research/author/critique/red-team depth.
- A higher tier adds the ideas, tasks, and fences that only emerge at the language or cross-`libs/` altitude.
- Higher tiers massage lower-tier items into cross-folder and cross-language alignment, widening scope or integration wherever it raises value.
- Any item formulated in isolation of its folder's ground-up code-doctrine integration is corrected or removed.
- A cross-language idea lands at its right touchpoint the same turn.
- Stages: research verified against live repo and package state, then implement, critique, red-team, reconcile-fix, verify on cross-file residuals.
- Ambitious synthesis is producer -> red-team -> merge, never a single agent.
- Every stage writes: a stage that only reads, classifies, or reports is a process defect.
- Review output is an applied fix receipt, never a ledger or deferred work list.
- Universal adversarial stance: every stage holds the work naive, shallow, or illusory until it survives an aggressive attack.
- Burden of proof is on the code: dense, confident-looking work is the prime suspect for hollowness.
- "Mature", "good enough", and a prior clean verdict are rejected self-assessments.
- A no-edit verdict is earned by an attack that finds nothing, never conceded on first read.
- A cross-batch dossier, map, or worklist anchors every ground claim by symbol name, never file:line.
- Concurrent batches shift or realize anchored lines before the consumer runs.
- A symbol anchor stays resolvable where a line anchor silently points at foreign content.
- Naivety is a defect on two orthogonal axes, both intolerable — COVERAGE and APPROACH.
- COVERAGE naivety — a thin slice of the concept: three obvious fields where the domain carries fifteen, a two-case family for a twenty-case domain.
- APPROACH naivety — hardcoded enumerated instances where a parameterized algorithmic owner generates the space.
- A fixed roster of styles, patterns, or variants is seed data feeding one generator, never the mechanism itself.
- Collapse freedom: every enumerated collapse-signal list in a workflow prompt is a floor, never the complete set.
- Repeated structure, parallel spelling, or enumerable family an algebra, table, fold, or generator can own is a collapse target beyond that floor.
- External-lib ultra-stacking at review: both `.api` tiers are enumerated in full and mined to operator depth at every stage.
- An admitted capability the concept admits but no owner exploits is a defect the review closes.
- A cited member that cannot be verified is a phantom the review deletes.
- Dual-axis review at critique and red-team grades every fence against route-owned doctrine on both axes — code quality and capability-completeness.
- Optimization is graded by performance and sophistication, never line count.
- Doctrine is the FLOOR, never the ceiling: critique and red-team hold every fence to full `docs/stacks/<language>/` adherence, then push past it.
- A fence that merely conforms is unfinished where a stronger form exists, and the expectation above the doctrine is the reviewer's own.
- Doc-craft grades against the authoring standard's review law.
- One campaign guard: fence-level rail-unification and density terms never surface as an `ARCHITECTURE` sub-domain name.
- Boundary integrity at critique and red-team: grade every concern against the boundaries `ARCHITECTURE.md` owns, fixing or recording each defect.
- Defect archetypes: a concern owned twice within a runtime, a folder mixing unrelated concerns, a concern scattered across three or four folders.
- A touchpoint coupled to a sibling's interior rather than an aligned seam in the folder `ARCHITECTURE.md` `[2]-[SEAMS]` map is a boundary defect.
- Coverage audit (IDEATE): task every manager/README mismatch — a package claimed but absent, or present but unregistered.
- Audit tasks also cover every missing `.api/<package>.md` catalogue, every newer or stronger package, and the per-language testing and tooling gaps.
- Catalog-alignment law: a `.api/` catalogue never redirects — a folder-tier file that points at or duplicates a substrate-tier catalogue is deleted.
- A package the substrate already carries is REGISTERED for the folder, never re-catalogued.
- Two-tier ownership — substrate or folder — is resolved by reading the substrate tier FIRST.
- A package admission or substrate hand-off updates its full touch-point set bi-directionally in one pass.
- That touch-point set is the `Rasm/CLAUDE.md` `[03]` roster; a lagging member is an alignment task.
- Rulings discipline: the campaign maintains a `RULINGS.md` decision registry every pass.
- Its admission bar, tier placement, `(none)`-section convention, and row anatomy follow the docgen rulings template.
- Every stage reads the owning folder, branch, cross-`libs/`, and touched `tests/` registries before re-deciding a settled question.
- A violation found against a ruling routes as a card — IDEA at the unifying altitude, TASK at landing grain — never an inline workaround.
- Law lives in the ruling row, actionable findings land in the pass's dossier, and critique mints cards from dossiers.
- Research-section discipline: realizing a research row moves its finding to the landing fence and deletes the row whole.
- A page's terminal `[RESEARCH]` section and its markers survive every pass under the docgen marker law.
- Card markers follow the file's own template comment.
- Model policy: assign by veracity need, never over-granularize.
- Lighter-model lanes: exploration, decompile reads, currency dossiers, throwaway-harness authoring.
- Capable-model lanes: every authoring, red-team, merge, alignment, cross-cutting, ideation lane.

## [04]-[SIBLING_BRANCHES]

Every campaign gives each branch its own concurrent allocation, additive never subtractive, never effort diverted from a sibling; branch peer-status and owner-set re-derivation are `ARCHITECTURE.md`'s.

Each branch runs its own aggressive ideation into its tiered `IDEAS.md`/`TASKLOG.md`, reading the sibling corpora for cross-pollination; the cross-language concert lands in the cross-`libs/` pool. Branch roles and the wire/companion seams bounding this cross-pollination are `ARCHITECTURE.md`.

## [05]-[PLAN_MODE_DISCIPLINE]

Plan mode exits only when every gate, fence, page, sub-domain, admission, catalogue, manifest edit, and refinement the campaign touches is decision-complete — no residual research deferred to an execution turn, no finding, idea, gap, or suggestion truncated or tossed. Each finding lands at its right tier and target; a finding without a home is a defect, not a discard.

Every addition is critiqued and red-teamed as it lands, and the whole plan and corpus are critiqued and red-teamed as one body before the plan finalizes.

A red-team or verify stage flips a status only against evidence — a spike or gap marked finalized only against a cited `.api` line or harness output, the `tools.assay` operator the rail: `api` for member truth, `docs check` for fence-bearing pages, `provision` for Forge toolchain facts, `bridge` for live-host scenario facts. Work is never marked done without evidence; a thin plan is an un-exhausted one.
