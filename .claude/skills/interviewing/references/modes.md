# [MODES]

Each mode is the operating loop specialized to one extraction; the card carries entry, grounding scope, staged procedure with per-stage exit tests, and what lands. Ephemeral working forms are declared inline; durable kinds route to their templates.

## [01]-[INTERVIEW]

- Enter: Requirements ambiguous, contradictory, or living in the user's head; a brief, spec, or roadmap about to be authored from a thin prompt.
- Ground: The axes the request touches, plus the user's starting position — what they explored, tried, and rejected.
- Procedure: Establish the starting point (exit: the dump adds no new fact). Frame the buckets and run blindspot or brainstorm first where their signals dominate (exit: interview holds only residual known unknowns). Run impact-ranked rounds under the question law, challenging every conflict (exit: two consecutive rounds change no ruling).
- Lands: The ruling record as working form; each settled ruling that a future session consults as law graduates to a `decision-record`; roadmap-altitude rulings compose a `roadmap-brief`.
- Terminate: Every ruling marked, no conflict open, the fold-back prompt emitted.

## [02]-[BLINDSPOT_PASS]

- Enter: Unfamiliar territory, a plan or corpus section about to seal, an explicit "what am I missing", or the unknown-unknowns bucket dominating the frame.
- Ground: The full axis catalog over the target — this mode is the grounding pass weaponized; delegation legs for a target beyond one reading budget.
- Procedure: Run each selected axis's probe and collect candidates (exit: every touched axis probed, clean axes recorded clean). Verify every candidate on disk (exit: zero unverified survivors). Rank survivors by blast radius and challenge the target's own settled lines with the assumptions check and prospective hindsight (exit: each finding carries anchor, consequence, and fold-back).
- Lands: A `blindspot-ledger`; zero findings is a legal result stated plainly with the probed axes listed. Findings from the capability-weakness and touch-point axes that state standing capability facts land as `capability-entry` rows beside the ledger.
- Terminate: The ledger seals when a cold read needs no transcript; the fold-back prompt folds the findings into the originating task.

## [03]-[BRAINSTORM]

- Enter: A named problem with multiple viable interventions and no committed direction; the unknown-knowns bucket dominating — the user will recognize the right direction only on sight.
- Ground: The corpus surfaces each candidate direction touches; the real constraint set that kills weak candidates early. Grounding feeds the frame — the constraint set and kills-early line — never the generation pool: seeds seen before generation narrow the category search.
- Procedure: Generate a free first pool across the tiers — patch, structural, rebuild — before any seed or prior-art exposure (exit: two tiers, distinct theses). Expand by forced category jumps — mechanism, goal, inversion, failure, scale, horizon, consumer — folding in seeds only after the free pool exists (exit: no two directions share a thesis). Attack each with disconfirmation, then score the wargame variant on weights declared before scoring (exit: the full field set per direction).
- Lands: A `direction-set` with the leading direction and the flip triggers that overturn it; the rendered comparison page is html-artifacts ground.
- Terminate: The user reacts to rendered directions, the ruling is recorded, rejected directions keep their reopen conditions.

## [04]-[QUIZ]

- Enter: Work about to merge or seal with comprehension of the change unproven.
- Ground: The diff's least-visited corners, the decisions that almost went the other way, the failure modes — never the summary.
- Procedure: Draw questions from the edges, each concrete and single-answer (exit: every question maps to a section or hunk that teaches it). Score against the key (exit: scored). Gate: a perfect score proceeds; anything less re-teaches from the mapped sections and re-quizzes with fresh questions (exit: perfect pass).
- Lands: The scored gate, ephemeral; a wrong answer that exposed a real design gap graduates as a `blindspot-ledger` finding.
- Terminate: Perfect pass or an explicit decision to hold the merge.

## [05]-[BUY_IN]

- Enter: A decision needing acceptance from a reviewer or stakeholder outside the loop.
- Ground: The evidence in the work — demo, spec, ruling record, implementation notes — and the objection surface of each audience.
- Procedure: Lead with the demo and compress the narrative (exit: the packet opens with the strongest evidence, not the history). Pre-answer the objections each audience raises — cost, risk, alternatives not taken, precedent — from evidence in the work, one lane per opposed audience (exit: every anticipated objection carries an evidence-backed answer). Name the asks and the sign-off list (exit: every ask has an owner).
- Lands: The packet, ephemeral; the accepted decision graduates to a `decision-record` and the packet dies.
- Terminate: Sign-off recorded or the objection that blocked it enters the ruling record as an open conflict.

## [06]-[TEACH_ME]

- Enter: The user cannot evaluate options or phrase intent — vague vocabulary, gesture-level requests, a domain where the user cannot yet judge what good looks like.
- Ground: The minimal concept set the pending decisions require, drawn from the corpus and the domain — never the full domain tour.
- Procedure: Build vocabulary interactively — concept, concrete example, the technical term (exit: the user restates their intent in the new terms). Check evaluation ability: the user ranks two contrasting examples and defends the ranking (exit: the ranking is defended in the taught terms). Hand the restated intent to the interview or brainstorm the teaching unblocked (exit: the pending decision is now phrasable).
- Lands: The vocabulary map — vague term, technical term, the decision it unlocks — ephemeral; it binds the follow-on mode the way the ruling record binds authoring.
- Terminate: The user's restated request needs no translation.
