# [STRONGEST_FORM]

One maximum-depth adversarial lens the broad correctness sweep cannot afford: every changed code fence is naive, shallow, or illusory until it survives attack, and the burden of proof is on the fence. Where the correctness review matches lines against a defect checklist, this pass runs the `libs/.planning/campaign-method.md` red-team counterfactual as a standing gate — it derives and names the stronger shape, never merely flags a conformance gap. Judge every fence against the owning `docs/stacks/<language>/` doctrine as the floor and `docs/standards/design-doctrine.md` as the universal law, then push past both; a fence that merely conforms is unfinished wherever a stronger form exists.

Hold each changed owner as its own target and hunt these classes, every finding naming file, anchor, the naive shape, and the concrete stronger form — the owner kind, the collapse, or the admitted-package operator — precise enough to transcribe:
- Counterfactual collapse: remove the fence's central assumption — the chosen owner kind, a hand-enumerated space, a call-site dispatch, a hand-rolled kernel — and derive the form without it. A flat `record`/`dataclass`/`interface` beside a generated or Schema owner sharing its identity regime, an enumerated arm ladder where a table, fold, or state-threaded dispatch owns the family, and a per-instance body where a parameterized generator spans the space are findings; the current shape also working is never a refutation.
- Coverage naivety: an owner modelling a thin slice of its concept — three obvious fields where the domain carries fifteen, a two-case family for a many-case domain, a missing attribute, sub-kind, state, relationship, invariant, or operation the domain implies. Zero current consumers never lowers the bar.
- Approach naivety: a fixed roster of styles, patterns, or variants hardcoded where it is seed data feeding one algebraic owner over named parameters.
- Library under-mining: a gap between an admitted package's operator depth and the fence that consumes it — a BCL or stdlib reflex, a hand-rolled loop, or a shallow call where the package's own deepest combinator, generated surface, or algebra owns the invariant. Both `.api/` tiers name the verified member; a claimed member that cannot be verified is a phantom to delete.
- Consumer overhead: orchestration, policy resolution, routing, retry, telemetry, or lifecycle pushed onto callers instead of internalized behind the entrypoint.
- Diff-of-the-next-feature: an owner not shaped for the family it absorbs next — the next case, dimension, or modality landing as a new surface rather than one declaration inside the owner with every consumer untouched or loudly broken.

Settled corpus law outranks intuition: a shape the owning page or `.api/` catalogue rules is never a finding. A pass that finds nothing after genuine attack states that verdict plainly and never manufactures a finding to fill the space.
