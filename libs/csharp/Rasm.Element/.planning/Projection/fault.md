# [ELEMENT_FAULTS]

The seam fault rail: `ElementFault` a closed `[Union]` band (band 2500) for the structural-graph, value-admission, projection, and content-verification failures every `Rasm.Element` entrypoint produces — `NodeAbsent` (a reference naming a `NodeId` the graph never declares), `RelationshipInvalid` (an edge whose endpoint node-kinds violate the seam's structural edge law, or a cyclic `Compose` ancestry), `DeltaConflict` (a put-existing, drop-absent, or duplicate-link state conflict), `ValueRejected` (the band-wide rail every typed-value smart-constructor across the seam value vocabulary fails onto), `ProjectionFailed` (a projector delta the structural law rejects, or a captured projector exception lowered at the boundary), and `AddressUnstable` (the `Projection/address#CONTENT_ADDRESS` `ContentAddress.Verify` re-hash dual finding a persisted node whose recomputed content id no longer equals its stored `NodeId` — the H7 tamper/corruption gate). The band is `Expected`-derived (`IValidationError<ElementFault>`) so band 2500 IS the `Expected` `Code` and a bare typed case lifts DIRECTLY onto the `Fin<T>`/`Validation<Error,T>` rail through the LanguageExt implicit `Error` conversion — no `.ToError()` lowering hop — each case carrying the kernel `Op` operation context and a `Category` telemetry projection. The legacy per-case-code + manual `Code`/`Message`/`ToError()` shape is the defect this owner closes: band 2500 is a `Code => FaultBand.Element` override over the parameterless kernel `Rasm.Domain.Expected` — the `[TABLES]` registry row through the generated implicit `SmartEnum`-to-`int` conversion — and the typed case IS the `Error`.

The band is one row on the in-process `Fin<T>` federation and never re-cases its neighbors: the `[TABLES]` `FaultBand` `[SmartEnum<int>]` registry — authored on THIS page, the cross-federation band-allocation owner every peer band's `Code` rebinds to — is the one roster of band integers and declaring owners, a duplicate integer failing the generated key lookup at type initialization, so band disjointness is type-enforced, never prose, and a cross-folder `Fin<T>` reads its origin from the band code. Two split laws bound the band: the seam's total `Switch` enforces ONLY the STRUCTURAL edge law onto `ElementFault`, the IFC-SEMANTIC legality riding the consumer's `Projection/projection#GRAPH_CONSTRAINT` `IGraphConstraint.Validate` `Validation<Error,Unit>` [M3] — never a sixth seam arm; and the content-address MINT projection is TOTAL, so `AddressUnstable`'s sole producer is the `ContentAddress.Verify` re-hash mismatch — never the byte projection, and never a non-finite value, which `ValueRejected` rejects at its admission gate.

Wire posture: HOST-NEUTRAL. `ElementFault` rides the `Fin<T>` rail every seam entrypoint returns and never sits between wire and rail — the `Graph/element#ELEMENT_GRAPH` `ElementGraph.Apply` (the validated delta-replay) and `Bake` fold, the authoring `Graph/delta#GRAPH_DELTA` `WorkingGraph.Apply`, the `Properties/quantity#MEASURE_VALUE` `MeasureValue.Of`/`OfCount`/`Sum` (the SI-native `OfSi` is TOTAL — `MeasureValue`, not `Fin` — so it carries no rail), the `Properties/property#PROPERTY_VALUE` `PropertyValue.Of`, the `Composition/acoustic#ACOUSTIC_FOLDS` `Acoustic.Of`, the `Composition/material#MATERIAL_COMPOSITION` `MaterialComposition.OfLayerSet`/`OfConstituentSet` and `Composition/material#MATERIAL_PROPERTY` `MaterialPropertySet.OfMechanical`/`OfOrthotropic`/`OfThermal`/`OfEnvironmental`/`OfCost` (the discipline vocabulary `Parse` factories — `FireRating`/`Currency`/`MeasurementBasis`/`ImpactCategory` — railing the unknown-token miss), the `Geospatial/reference#GEO_REFERENCE` `GeoReference.Admit`, the `Geospatial/coverage#COVERAGE_NODE` `CoverageGrid.Of`/`RasterSampleType.Parse`, the `Relations/relation#EDGE_ALGEBRA` `CardinalPoint.Of`, the `Projection/projection#PROJECTION_CONTRACT` `Assemble` fold, and the `Projection/address#CONTENT_ADDRESS` `ContentAddress.Verify` re-hash gate (`Fin<Unit>` single-node, `Validation<Error,Unit>` snapshot sweep). The TOTAL surfaces carry no fault rail and never mint a case: `Projection/address#CONTENT_ADDRESS` `ContentAddress.Of`/`OfGraph` and `Graph/element#NODE_MODEL` `Node.ToCanonicalBytes` are total (canonicalizing rather than faulting), and `Graph/element#ELEMENT_GRAPH` `ElementGraph.Topology`/the `Element` read accessors are total. The seam carries no fault-band string-comparer accessor: `ElementFault` dispatches through its generated total `Switch`, keys no `FrozenDictionary`, and a `[KeyMemberComparer]` on the fault is the deleted form.

## [03]-[RESEARCH]

- [BAND_ALIGNMENT]: the `Expected`-derivation grounds against the kernel `Rasm.Domain` `Expected` (`abstract record Expected : Error` with a parameterless `protected` ctor, a `virtual Category => "Fault"`, and the inherited `virtual Error.Code => 0`/`abstract Error.Message`), the kernel `Op` `[ValueObject<string>]` (`Op.Of` caller-member-name), the kernel union-ops generator law (strictly opt-in through `[GenerateUnionOps]` — a fault band stays unmarked, exactly as the kernel `Fault` band does, so no suppression marker exists or is needed), the Thinktecture `IValidationError<TValidationError>` contract the `[Union]` generator emits, and the production `UiFault` band (the `using Expected = Rasm.Domain.Expected;` alias + the `…Case` records + the per-case static factories). Band 2500 is a `Code => FaultBand.Element` override — the `[TABLES]` registry row consumed through the generated implicit `SmartEnum`-to-`int` conversion, never a `.Value` spelling or a magic literal (the kernel base carries no `Code`/`Message` ctor slot — the `Expected(string, int, Option<Error>)` ctor belongs to the OTHER `LanguageExt.Common.Expected`, which carries no `Category`, so the `base(detail, 2500, None)` form is the defect). The shape decision is the homogeneous-case form the rebuilt federation sibling `BimFault` realizes: a shared `private XFault(Op key, string detail)` base, `Message => Detail`, and a per-case `Category` override — so `ElementFault`'s six homogeneous `(Op Key, string Detail)` cases collapse identically, `Message => Detail` once rather than a six-arm `Message => Switch(…)` whose arms all return `c.Detail` (the repeated-dispatch-arm collapse trigger). The production `UiFault` band keeps a central `Message => Switch(…)` only because its cases are HETEROGENEOUS — `MissingScopeCase(string Field)` carries no `Op`, `CancelledCase(Op Op)` carries no `Detail`, and each arm formats a distinct sentence — so the central switch there is the form the heterogeneity forces, not the homogeneous-case form `ElementFault` takes. Arm-specificity is `error.IsType<ElementFault.ValueRejectedCase>()` (the `Error.IsType<E> where E : Error` probe — there is no `Error.Is<E>()`, the `Error.Is(Error)` overload takes an `Error` argument), the `Category` string surfacing through the kernel `FaultExtensions.Category(this Error)` (`error switch { Expected e => e.Category, _ => "Fault" }`) for telemetry. This is the C# pattern law "fault = a closed `[Union]` deriving from `Expected`" the rebuilt sibling bands already realize, the `[TABLES]` registry the one roster a telemetry reader bands by.
- [CONSTRAINT_SPLIT]: the structural edge law the seam's total `Switch` enforces (`Graph/delta#GRAPH_DELTA` `LegalLink`/`LegalAssign` endpoint-kind legality over the `Relations/relation#EDGE_ALGEBRA` `Endpoints`) rails `RelationshipInvalid`, while the IFC-semantic legality a consumer layers rides the `Projection/projection#GRAPH_CONSTRAINT` `IGraphConstraint.Validate` `Validation<Error,Unit>` [M3] — the two are disjoint by design so the seam carries no IFC vocabulary and the `Rasm.Bim`-implemented constraint carries no structural-graph mechanics. The `Expected` + `IValidationError<ElementFault>` shape keeps the typed arm recoverable through `Error.Combine`: the `IGraphConstraint` accumulation folds each independent violation into a `ManyErrors` whose `HasCode`/`IsType`/`Filter` overrides each recurse over the inner `Seq<Error>` (`HasCode` is `Errors.Exists(e => e.Code == code)`, `IsType<E>` is `Errors.Exists(e => e.IsType<E>())`, `Filter<E>` is `Errors.Choose(…)` over the matching arms), so `error.IsType<ElementFault.RelationshipInvalidCase>()` and `error.HasCode(2500)` survive the combine and `error.Filter<ElementFault.RelationshipInvalidCase>()` extracts the matching arms — where a bare `Error.New(2500, message)` from a `.ToError()` hop would erase the arm into an untyped message.
- [TOTAL_ADDRESS]: the content-address projection (`Projection/address#CONTENT_ADDRESS` `ContentAddress.Of`/`OfGraph`, `Graph/element#NODE_MODEL` `Node.ToCanonicalBytes`) is TOTAL by design — the `Projection/address#CANONICAL_WRITER` `CanonicalWriter` canonicalizes `-0.0`→`0.0`, every `NaN`→one quiet-NaN pattern, and ±∞ to their canonical IEEE bits, and quantizes every measure to `Header.Tolerance`, so a sign-of-zero or a payload-NaN never forks the hash and the projection is byte-stable across the C#/Python/TypeScript runtimes sharing the one `XxHash128` seed-zero rail; a faulting address would regress the H7 cross-runtime parity guarantee (the `ToCanonicalBytes` result is also the non-rooted `NodeId.Content` mint and the `Rasm.Persistence` `StructuralMerge` edge/node key, neither of which can be a `Fin`), and the `Associate` edge's unset-`NaN` `ReferenceExtent` sentinel depends on the writer's `NaN` canon. Every non-finite scalar is therefore rejected at its VALUE-ADMISSION gate (`MeasureValue.Of`/`OfCount`, `Acoustic.Of`, the material/property/geo admissions) as `ValueRejected` BEFORE it can reach the byte projection, so the MINT path (`ContentAddress.Of`/`OfGraph`/`ToCanonicalBytes`) never produces `AddressUnstable` — routing a non-finite through `AddressUnstable` is the deleted reading, the "reject a non-finite value" capability preserved by `ValueRejected` at admission; `AddressUnstable` rides the `ContentAddress.Verify` re-hash dual ALONE (a recomputed content id != the stored `NodeId`), the H7 corruption gate the total writer's mint can never hit but a tampered or corrupted persisted node can.
