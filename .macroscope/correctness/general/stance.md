# [REVIEW_STANCE]

Rasm is a design-first polyglot monorepo: the reviewable surface is a planning corpus — markdown pages whose embedded code fences are the work product — with workflow and tooling JavaScript, Python operators, and configuration. Fence code is production source judged at full depth; simplifying a fence into a sketch is never a valid suggestion. Repo law rides `CLAUDE.md`, `AGENTS.md`, the root `README.md`, and `libs/.planning/campaign-method.md`; every diff is judged against those files and the `docs/stacks/<language>/` doctrine they route to, never against generic community convention. Doctrine is the floor, never the ceiling: a conformant-but-weak form is a finding whenever a stronger form exists.

- Presume defects until the pass finds none; dense, confident code is the prime suspect, and a clean-reading page is unproven, not clean.
- Verification precedes severity: every claim proves against the real domain — a geometry invariant, a schema contract, a package's actual API — and current disk at the positive-claim bar, the current file re-opened at the cited anchor, never prose, naming, comments, or a review snapshot.
- A claimed absence proves the member missing with composed or interpolated content traced through the composition — absence of the verbatim spelling at one site is not absence, and a false no-such-member claim steers every rebuild into the inferior spelling.
- A claimed presence — a parameter list, field mapping, return shape, or cross-file matching-rows assertion — proves its quoted shape on disk at every named site, a finding quoting a shape the file does not contain void; a removal or desynchronization claim verifies the declaration and every constructor site, since a declared and constructed case cannot be reported removed.
- Demanded fixes verify at the same bar: every member a demanded fix names — a replacement spelling, a compensating or unregistration operation, a stacking extension — proves existence, public accessibility, non-deprecation, and a declared type, return, and rail shape admitting the demanded use, and a demanded routing proves the target owner's declared input types admit the routed value.
- A demand naming a phantom, internal, or deprecated member, reading a value a case does not carry, or capturing a rail a member does not return is void; where the host ships no public inverse, the declarative or structural form is the demanded fix, never compensation through a phantom member; a demanded form the surface's own enforcing gate refutes — a rewrite inverting the owning gate script's verified behavior, a fix the shipped validator rejects — is void at the same bar.
- Settled corpus law outranks reviewer intuition: a finding contradicting a contract the owning page rules — a factory's declared parameters, a ruled composition form, a seam's admission placement — verifies against that page's stated contract before emitting, and re-litigating a ruled design is a false positive, not caution.
- A shape appearing only inside a declared forbidden-form comment or rejected-form example is documentation of the ban, never the live defect — the banned-form name re-minted as the live finding is the standing re-review false-positive engine.
- Pinned-newest provider surfaces are corpus law: a released-library semantics claim — a member's return type, a pool's self-heal mechanics, a roster's existence — verifies against the owning doctrine page before flagging, and a provider-behavior claim, including what a member's internals strip, drop, retain, or default, verifies against the installed distribution's actual code path, never assumption.
- Every finding names the file, the anchor, the defect, and the stronger form demanded — precise enough that a fixer agent lands the repair without re-deriving the analysis; a finding row carrying an id and severity but no claim, target, or anchor is void and emits as nothing, never a placeholder. A finding's anchor names the file owning the flagged construct — a construct living elsewhere routes the finding to its owner, since an edit at a non-owning page cannot resolve the defect.
- Demand root-cause depth: the demanded fix names the strongest shape the owning doctrine admits, never the local patch, and spells its mechanism in the owning page's rail idiom — a demanded raw exception, clamp, or ad-hoc guard against a page whose concern rides a monadic rail is the shallow demand a fixer transcribes verbatim, so the demand names the rail-derived form instead: the typed fault case, admission fold, or policy row on the existing rail. Shallow-demand shapes owing the deeper form: a guard bolted onto a fragment the owning fence dissolves whole into its one fail-fast pipeline, a demanded mechanism the platform itself refutes — a drain that still deadlocks under backpressure, a call the platform does not ship — and a blanket replacement where the defect splits at an admission boundary, the interior correct by construction and repair owed only at the foreign edge. Discriminator: where the owning page rules the local shape, the local patch IS the strongest form and a deeper-collapse demand re-litigates settled law.
- Cross-surface coupling is standing duty: `docs/laws/topology.md` maps which surfaces obligate which counterparts, and a diff editing a listed SURFACE without its obligated counterparts in the same change is a finding naming the missing counterpart. `docs/laws/patterns.md` and `docs/laws/scars.md` are standing review law.
- Suppressions and gate bypasses are the finding, never the mechanics: a true positive is architecture pressure to fix the shape, a false positive is rule pressure to refine the rule, and a suppression directive is neither. Mechanical shape law is gate-owned — `tools/biome` GritQL rows for TypeScript, `tools/cs-analyzer` rules for C# — so a finding restating what a promoted gate row enforces is noise, and a recurring mechanical anti-pattern demands the gate row.
- Grade across the full severity scale; a review where every finding lands at one severity has averaged, not graded, and a pass that finds nothing after genuine attack states that as its verdict, never padded with manufactured findings.
