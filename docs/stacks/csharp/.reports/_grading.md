# [GRADING]

Companion artifact to the doc-stack prompt set. The orchestrator installs a verbatim copy at the workspace root (`<GRADING>`) when a stack's first pipeline begins; every stage's grade answers to this file. Concept pages never cite it.

## [1]-[MECHANICS]

- A grade is the MINIMUM across the ladder's axes, stepped at 0.1; the mean is recorded for trend only and never gates anything.
- Bars: 9.2 min-axis admits a stage; 9.5 min-axis finalizes a snippet set — enforced at the snippet-refinement convergence and re-checked at the corpus sweep. Prose and research stay at 9.2 throughout.
- Automatic fails override every axis score; a file carrying one does not clear at any number.
- Grading is honest, critical, harsh, fair: a real seven is reworked until it clears, never relabeled; a 9.2 is never given for convenience, and a clear is never withheld for nit-picking.
- Producer grades admit; cold grades decide. A cold grader gives no weight to the producer's self-grade or effort, and re-derives the score from the artifact alone.
- Convergence is a pass that ends with zero edits AND every unit at or above the bar; the final pass is always verification-only, and manufactured change in a refinement pass is itself a defect.

## [2]-[RESEARCH_LADDER]

Findings files, bedrocks, and distillates (initial, hygiene, consolidate, divergent, distill).

| [AXIS]         | [6]                                  | [8]                                          | [9]                                                       | [9.5+]                                                                 |
| :------------- | :----------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| RICHNESS       | covers the obvious surface           | covers the lane's breadth                     | breadth plus the interactions between findings              | nothing material at the lane's altitude is missing                       |
| VERACITY       | plausible, unverified                | load-bearing claims verified, sources current | every claim verified against installed source or live docs | adversarially verified — each claim survives an attempt to refute it     |
| DENSITY        | no filler                            | every bullet carries one decision             | related findings fused, nothing repeats                     | nothing removable without losing a distinct fact                         |
| ADVANCEMENT    | correct but writable from memory     | one non-obvious mechanism or trap per group   | the advancement floor holds everywhere                      | the deepest verified layer the domain supports; an expert learns per group |
| LANE_RELEVANCE | on-topic with drift at the edges     | inside the lane, ASSUMES respected            | OWNS-exact, zero lateral material                           | every entry advances this lane's concern and could live nowhere else      |

- The advancement floor: an entry survives only when it carries a non-obvious mechanism, a verified trap or hazard, a law stated with its boundary, or a collapse fusing two or more findings into a richer rule. A table-stakes spine with one advanced edge keeps the edge and sheds the spine.
- Automatic fails: RF-1 invented or unverified API shape (arbiter: installed package source, then the compile harness); RF-2 bedrock re-tread in a divergent or distilled file; RF-3 lateral drift — material a sibling lane's OWNS or a finalized page already owns; RF-4 meta-commentary, sourcing, version, or date residue.

## [5]-[DRIFT_CHECKS]

Every grader, every ladder, regardless of stage:

- Lateral drift: material whose strongest claim belongs to a sibling lane's OWNS or a finalized page's territory.
- Re-tread: restating a lower layer — bedrock in a divergent, divergent in a distillate, finalized law in a page.
- Table-stakes survival: an entry below the advancement floor that a prior pass should have cut.
- Nuance loss: a merge that averaged instead of unioned — any source variant's edge missing from the merged entry.
- Region duplication: a demonstration or card concern the ledger already assigns to another owner.
- Convergence theater: manufactured change in a refinement pass, or a verification pass that rubber-stamps below-bar units.
