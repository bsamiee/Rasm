## [00]-[SHARED_LAW]

### [VERDICT]

The corpus is naive with ILLUSORY DEPTH: pages that gesture at capability without owning the generative machinery underneath. The four proofs, each independently fatal to a world-class claim:

1. **The single production entry is fiction.** `core/plan` `ArtifactPipeline` is constructed by ZERO pages; its `Work[ArtifactReceipt]` contract is structurally unsatisfiable against the six incompatible producer return shapes that actually exist (`RuntimeRail[ContentKey]` | `[ArtifactReceipt]` | `[tuple[ContentKey, ArtifactReceipt]]` | `[Result[...]]` | `[tuple[ContentKey, Evidence]]` | `[Block[Result[...]]]`); live parallel batch rails run in `document/emit` (`produced`), `document/report` (`rendered`), `core/format` (`_fanned`), and per-instance `of()` batches. Every `[SINGLE_PRODUCTION_ENTRY]` claim in the corpus is prose.
2. **The color foundation is orphaned.** No page imports `graphic/color/derive`'s `Colorimetry`/`ColorOp`; the palette that actually flows is a bare NDArray alias minted in `visualization/chart/spec` and imported by four DRAWING pages, plus a third alias in `scene/render`, plus literal hex in `drawing/schedule`. The contrast/CVD/gamut law the derive page owns is unreachable.
3. **Two text engines coexist.** The typography plane (uharfbuzz shaping, bidi, Knuth-Plass, COLRv1) is fully consumed only by `document/model` and `media/subtitle`; the entire drawing/diagram annotation plane runs on ad-hoc `ziafont`/`ziamath` calls — six pages hand-build math typesetting and glyph outlining beside the live owners, `drawing/annotate` decodes a `PositionedGlyphRun` then DISCARDS the positions and re-shapes, and ISO 3098 lettering never sees script itemization, fallback, or kerning-correct shaping.
4. **The section-fill system is eleven borrowed names.** `drawing/standard` `_HATCH` maps material → hardcoded ACAD pattern names with hand-tuned magic (`ANSI31` doing double duty for STEEL and GLASS); no pattern generator exists, no SVG-side hatch geometry exists anywhere, and `drawing/schedule` hand-rolls crosshatch swatches with invented hex because the fill system cannot serve a legend.

The corpus also ships VERIFIED-BROKEN paths: `visualization/table`'s default polars render raises on `fmt_*(columns=)`/`tab_stub(groupname_col=)` (proven by `drawing/schedule`'s own `[BLOCKED]` card; `delivery/register` silently hits the same wall), and `media/analysis` imports three functions (`render_png`, `montage`, `frame_similarity`) that no page defines. What IS sound: the 22-case `ArtifactReceipt` family (one violation: derive's parallel `ColorReceipt` rail), single-owner vocabularies (`Discipline`/`ScaleRatio`/`Terminator`/`SheetId`/`PositionedGlyphRun`/`TablePlan` each declared exactly once), and the exchange/media/package planes (8-9 grade). The rot is structural — unwired seams, wrong-owner picks, hand-rolled geometry beside live owners, intra-plane duplication — and no per-page cold pass fixes it. Architecture first.
