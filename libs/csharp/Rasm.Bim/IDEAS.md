# [RASM_BIM_IDEAS]

The forward pool of higher-order concepts for the host-neutral BIM-and-exchange engine. `[1]-[OPEN]` holds active ideas as cards; `[2]-[CLOSED]` records a finished or dropped idea with a one-line disposition.

OPEN contains `ACTIVE` work and `QUEUED` next-up work in logical sequence; `BLOCKED` keeps open but non-actionable work; `CLOSED` separates finished `COMPLETE` items from unimplemented `DROPPED` items.
