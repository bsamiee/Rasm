The Compute numeric spine is genuinely world-class where it composes the admitted heavy substrate (blas.md `LinearProvider`+ATen+`SpectralResult`+`OnlineStat` = 9/10; optimizer.md 11-kernel `OptimizerKind` with faithful hand-rolled CMA-ES/PSO/SA = 9/10; signal.md substrate-integrated FFT/FIR/IIR = 8/10).
