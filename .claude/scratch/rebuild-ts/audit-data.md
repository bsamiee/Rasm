# [AUDIT_DATA] — line-by-line doctrine audit of `libs/typescript/data`

Grade of every design-page fence against `docs/stacks/typescript` (README + 12 concept pages, read in full) and the campaign law. Sixteen laws stated: FLOW — `EXPRESSION_SPINE` (all domain logic expression-shaped on the rail; no statement/`let`/`push`), `BOUNDARY_ADMISSION` (decode once at the edge; interior total, never re-validates, never sees `null`), `CAPABILITY_CHANNEL` (dependency is a Tag satisfied by the Layer graph). SHAPE — `SCHEMA_AUTHORITY` (Schema owner is the one shape authority; a hand type beside it is the defect), `SHAPE_BUDGET` (one deep owner ≈ one-fifth naive shape count; brands in fields), `DEEP_SURFACES` (few rich exports, `_`-interior, terminal EXPORTS block), `MODAL_ARITY` (one entrypoint discriminates on input value, never suffix/knob), `ANTICIPATORY_COLLAPSE` (shape for the family, diff-of-next-feature is one row). DERIVATION — `VOCABULARY_VALUES` (one `as const` table, secondary surfaces derive), `DERIVED_TYPES` (`typeof`/`keyof typeof`/indexed access; no hand parallel), `SEMANTIC_NAMING`. MATERIAL — `LIBRARY_DEPTH` (Effect ecosystem is stdlib; use the deepest owned combinator), `INLINE_COMPOSITION` (wrapping/resilience/telemetry attach at the owner declaration). INTEGRATION — `ROOT_REBUILD` (weave capability into the owner), `ONE_HOP_RESOLUTION` (no alias/forwarding hops), `COMPOSED_IMPLEMENTATION` (compose page owners; flat code is surface sprawl).

Verdict up front: the corpus is architecturally strong on vocabulary-table collapse, dialect-honesty, capability fail-closed, and content identity. Its systemic weaknesses are (1) pervasive **untyped `Connection.Row` reads** that violate the folder's own `SqlSchema` law and `effect-sql.md:159`; (2) **hand-assembled SQL strings** in `search` that are exactly the "naive SQL" the campaign bans; (3) **resilience declared but never composed** — `ObjectFault.policy`/`OlapFault` carry retry intent no owner reads; (4) **no `Machine` actors** where the substrate admits them and the state genuinely outlives a traversal (drain daemon, fact rail); (5) **runtime DDL mutation** in `search`/`fold` contradicting the folder's own DDL-split boundary; (6) **`throw` in a pure constructor** (`evolve`). Ranked folder verdict at the tail.
