Binding: a capability names an ability independent of its implementation — the `Owner` field carries the implementation fact, so renames touch one field. `Refuses` names the attractive capability the owner must never absorb and the surface that holds it instead, spelling a forbidden coupling as owned law; it is omitted only when no neighbor tempts the owner. `Edges` stay at unit grain; member-level detail belongs to the owner's own page. `Gaps` is the load-bearing field for elicitation: an empty gaps field asserts the capability is fully exploited, and that assertion is checked, not assumed.
