Binding: the leader composes identity and lifecycle — advancement edits the status token alone, and supersession names the successor id in the superseded record's own leader, so the old record always points forward. A record standing `[PROPOSED]` past its deciding session is resolved or dropped by the next pass; a proposed status is a question, never a resting state. `Rejected` repeats per rejected option that stays useful; a field deciding nothing for a record is omitted, never filled. `Premises` marks are `fact` (anchored), `assumption` (carries its invalidating condition), or `inference` (names its premises); a record with no unproven premise omits the field.
