# [RASM_RULINGS]

Per-folder decision registry for the geometry kernel — the settled rulings agents re-litigate for lack of a home. A row is one decision with the one why that guards its re-litigation: a package admission or rejection, an owner-choice discriminant, a collapse refusal naming the guarantee the denser form loses, a structure retirement, or a folder-learned working law. Append-mostly; a row earns its seat while its why stays homeless, and dies only when fact and why both live at one durable surface.

## [01]-[PACKAGES]

- QuikGraph KEEP — do NOT re-reject as "unmaintained": MS-PL, the dominant graph library with no maintained successor, algorithmically complete for topo-sort/BFS/DAG; "unmaintained" is chronology-only, not a capability gap. Its core package alone suffices — no companions; reopens only on a maintained successor owning the same algorithm set under the license gate.
- Kernel admits only pure-managed, no-native-asset leaves — a package carrying a native/RID runtime asset or a host-engine binding homes at its consuming sibling, never `Rasm.csproj`; the discriminant is managed purity, not geometry domain, so a managed leaf still enters only on a live kernel consumer.
- geometry3Sharp and the MathNet MKL/OpenBLAS providers are NOT kernel references — those pins carry sibling debt for the L2/L3 consumers (geometry3Sharp co-consumed by Bim/Fabrication, the native providers by the compute lane), so a kernel author reaching for geometry3Sharp's DDG surface is re-adding sibling debt.

## [02]-[SHAPE]

- One synchronous hook capsule, not two shapes — discriminant: the kernel `Op` is a value object with a synchronous catch funnel, not an effect monad, so a second effect-threaded capsule type (the AppHost `IO`-shaped fire) is the rejected form; an effect-rail caller lifts `Fire` at its own composition seam instead.
- `SampleMoment.UpperCovariance` and `SymmetricMatrix.Upper` share one packed-upper layout contract — identical index arithmetic lets `CloudKernel.CovarianceOf` hand the moment triangle straight into `SymmetricMatrix.Of` with no repack; nothing types an `Arr<double>` layout, so a single-owner "simplification" or a defensive repack at the handoff is the move that corrupts every spectral consumer type-invisibly, and the layout changes only at both owners in one pass.
- Packed-upper triangular addressing has ONE arithmetic mint — `SymmetricMatrix.FlatIndex` promotes to `internal` and every packed-upper producer (`SampleMoment`'s indexer, `Lm.PackedIndex`) delegates to it; a hand-copied index formula is the drift the mirror-law prose names without enforcing, and the `Lm` copy retains only its checked `long` widening at the call boundary.
- Inexact Newell area-vector folding has one floor owner on the numerics atoms surface — every ring, panel, and frame consumer composes it, while the exact `DominantOf` carrier never yields its Newell vector (exact and inexact stay two owners by decision); a page-local Newell re-mint is the deleted form the `DominantAxis` collapse already cured once.
