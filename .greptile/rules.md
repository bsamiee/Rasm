# Review charter — Rasm

Repo law rides the files.json reads: `CLAUDE.md` owns execution and code-generation law, `AGENTS.md` owns the load order and engineering contract, the root `README.md` owns workspace topology, and `libs/.planning/campaign-method.md` is the review standard for all design-corpus work under `libs/`. Judge every diff against those files and the `docs/stacks/<language>/` doctrine they route to, never against generic community convention. Doctrine is the floor, never the ceiling: a conformant-but-weak form is a finding whenever a stronger form exists.

A design corpus is the reviewable surface — markdown planning pages whose embedded code fences are the work product — plus workflow and tooling JavaScript and configuration. Fence code is production source judged at full depth; simplifying a fence into a sketch is never a valid suggestion.

## [01]-[STANCE]

- Presume defects until the review finds none. Dense, confident code is the prime suspect: density earns scrutiny proportional to the claims it packs, and a clean-reading page is unproven, not clean.
- Verify every claim against the real domain — a geometry invariant, a schema contract, a package's actual API — never by ratifying the prose, the naming, or a comment's assertion that the code does what it says — and against current disk state, so a finding quoting an argument, literal, or shape the file does not contain is void. A claim that a member does NOT exist verifies at the same bar as a positive citation — the current file re-opened and the member proven missing before emission, since a stale review snapshot never outranks disk and a finding built on one is void, not low-severity: a false absence claim steers every future rebuild into the inferior spelling the owning catalog rejects.
- Settled corpus law outranks reviewer intuition: a finding contradicting a contract the owning page rules — a factory's declared budget parameters, a ruled composition form, a seam's admission placement, a diagram template's exclusions — is wrong until that page is read and the claim verified against its stated contract; re-litigating a ruled design is a false positive, not caution. Pinned-newest provider surfaces are corpus law, so a released-library semantics claim — a member's return type, a pool's self-heal mechanics, a roster's existence — verifies against the owning doctrine page before flagging: the page read precedes severity assignment, and a claim contradicting the page is never emitted.
- Standing hunts run as active searches against every diff, each presuming the gap until the pass proves otherwise — a diff none of them examined is unreviewed, not clean.
- Stacking census: enumerate every touched fence's composed member set against the folder-tier `.api/` catalogue, the language substrate tier `libs/<lang>/.api/`, and the central manifest (`Directory.Packages.props`, `pyproject.toml`, `pnpm-workspace.yaml`). A hand-rolled body re-deriving what a catalogued member owns at operator depth is a finding naming that member; a rail-verified host or package member that strengthens a touched owner and sits unconsumed is a finding whose extension lands as a row, case, or member on that owner, never a parallel surface; a page composing only shallow members of a package whose catalogue reaches deeper is under-stacked until each shallow call proves itself the deepest applicable form the package ships. Declared capability is consumed capability: a capability noun with no anchoring fence member, a receipt nothing mints, or a policy row nothing consumes is decorative density demanding the consuming member or the deletion; a policy column consumed only by a cache key, receipt, or log annotates rather than governs — demand the governing arm; an arm that cannot honor a policy field refuses the combination loudly before provider work — silent weakening is the finding.
- Strata leverage: the owning `ARCHITECTURE.md` strata row names the owners a folder composes — a fence re-deriving capability a designated lower-stratum owner carries demands composition of that owner, an owner minted at a higher stratum for a concern a lower-stratum owner already carries is duplication the lower owner absorbs, and two sibling consumers composing one stratum owner through divergent spellings converge on one composed form; every finding names the strata row, the owner, and the uncomposed or duplicated member.
- Parameterization hunts in both directions: a literal that encodes a decision or recurs across sites becomes a policy value or vocabulary row, and a stringly key, path, or per-site spelling of one correspondence derives from the owning symbol; inversely, a knob set whose combinations the body re-derives collapses into policy rows, table dispatch, or a denser polymorphic owner with defaults derived once.
- Shape collapse is an active search, never a passive style note: a case family accreted flat collapses into one polymorphic owner the moment siblings share an identity regime, admission path, payload timing, or consumer — a flat record beside a generated owner, a parallel dataclass or per-mode model beside a tagged union, an interface or hand-written union beside a Schema owner or vocabulary table — and two parallel types differing by one axis of variation are one type carrying a discriminant.
- Entry surfaces are designed artifacts judged whole: verb siblings, arity twins, mode flags, `get`/`getMany`/`getBy` families, and exports grown per capability demand absorption into one polymorphic entry per rail discriminating on input shape; a helper with exactly one caller, no plausible second consumer, and no meaning in isolation inlines into that caller, and a consumer forced to sequence a module's internals marks the weak boundary the entrypoint absorbs.
- Tenfold anticipation: a concept modelled at a fraction of the axes its domain carries is under-designed now — the finding names the missing attributes, sub-kinds, states, relationships, invariants, or operations — and capability the owning charter implies but the surface cannot express demands the axis as rows, cases, or members inside the existing owner; zero current consumers never lowers the bar.
- Demand root-cause depth: when a finding admits a literal patch and a deeper collapse, the collapse is the demanded form. Proven classes worth hunting: required-field threading dropped by signature migrations, finiteness admission before range checks — family-wide across the policy roster, zero-sentinel law stated beside the guard — identity preimages omitting output-affecting inputs, match/dispatch totality, frozen-payload discipline — mutable defaults, unsorted iteration reaching egress — per-stream timestamp re-basing, resource custody on every exit path, archive and metadata bomb gates, deterministic egress ordering. Execution policy and display labels stay outside a content preimage — a timeout or retry budget changes when work fails, never the bytes a success produces, and a label inside the preimage is a collision defect; a security identity takes a cryptographic digest, never a speed hash, and a composite id rides a canonical codec, never a hand-rolled join or quote scheme injective on one ambiguity axis.
- End-state depth guides the demanded form: a guard fixed at one site signals the same guard missing at every sibling site — demand the shared owner, never the local check; a defect in one arm of a family is evidence about every arm — demand the family fix; N siblings re-spelling one lifecycle machine over a shared rail — mint, custody hand-off, multi-product harvest, failure release, entry guard — demand that machinery as generic members on the rail owner with the siblings collapsed to one-line compositions, hand-maintained congruence invariants becoming structural inside the one generic builder, repeated dispose-on-failure blocks collapsing to one extension on the rail's result type, and a guard present on some family entries but absent on siblings is the latent defect the absorption fixes.
- Evidence read at use-time belongs at construction — demand mint-time binding: an aggregate owning a correlating mint factory admits only through it, since a direct construction over complete-looking parts erases the correlation the factory binds, and a derived value keeps one authority, so a configuration surface re-asserting what a construction factory already derives silently overwrites the mint, a hand-maintained mirror of a roster another owner carries — a slot enum shadowing a case list, hand-numbered keys shadowing host ordinals, per-case traits re-spelled beside the owning row — derives from that roster, and an evidence or receipt parameter every call site hardwires to one value is false evidence — the field derives from the run fact it reports or deletes.
- An invalid state becomes unspellable rather than guarded at each use, and a flagged value check resolves to one of three classes before severity — interior re-validation of an admitted value is deleted-form residue whose deletion is the demand (generated-enum instances and value-struct payloads sit in this class by construction: a generated smart enum admits no invalid instance and a value struct spells no null), a default-ghost storage seam on a value-object owner the language can zero-init past its admission factory is mandated and never redundant, and a host-crossing read or import owes legitimate admission because nulls and malformed shapes are real there — with misassignment across the three the dominant false-positive engine; two paths producing one product converge on one seam, and the unifying diff deletes the dead hop; a missing check on a protocol surface signals the incomplete fold — demand the whole admission; a provider-behavior claim — including what a member's internals strip, drop, retain, or default — verifies against the installed distribution's actual code path, never assumption; canonicalization lands at intake so every consumer reads one regime.
- A residual gated against a zero ceiling is non-negative by construction — absolute deviation from target — or a degenerate input passes the clamp; a clamp over a caller defect launders it into false evidence, so typed admission precedes normalization and a shared evidence fold defines its empty-input value explicitly, and a builder that drops a record on a failed reference lookup launders the dangling reference past its own integrity pass — the miss materializes as typed evidence the pass consumes, never a silent drop; a comment naming a root or anchor the code does not use marks a live defect — the owning root resolves once and threads as a parameter, never ambient reads; a dispatch table indexed by foreign material derives its membership admission from the table itself, never an enumerated reject-list, and an allowed-set beside hand-spelled per-member writes is two enumerations of one roster — one delegate-column table owns both, the gate deriving from `ContainsKey` and the writes folding over its rows; recursive expansion over untrusted sources carries container-identity cycle rejection and a node budget on the expand arm — one global identity set and one global remaining budget across the whole recursive forest, never per-branch guards; a seam raising a typed fault spells the fault whole as the primary argument with context on the note channel, and multiple raise sites collapse onto one reconstruction owner; failure-diagnostic side channels beside an empty or failed primary result ride the typed fault or receipt on that branch — success-only evidence binding discards the explanation where it decides; evidence and telemetry labels ride domain-owned prefixes, never a sibling domain's; a filter defined by negation silently admits new states, so the surviving set is a named positive constant, and admission over foreign carriers is a closed pattern family of admissible shapes with one refusing arm — never a negative check list.
- Host-mutation choreography carries proven end-states worth demanding: the complete mutation plan derives against the un-mutated record so refusal leaves state untouched, one terminal admission bind over the construction switch keeps the admission authority single, and a scoped entry admits its request before acquiring the resource grant; pre-mutation total-state capture yields one compensation rail across all sources, restores landing in reverse order with restore faults appended to the primary fault, and a bracket owner composes suppression, body, restoration, and terminal flush on that one rail — never parallel failure paths; where clear-all is the host's only rollback member, the owner's journal turns batch failure into clear-and-replay, unclonable host state stages on the host-supplied copy — prove there, then commit — with the inverse misread the standing false positive: a duplicate-revise-then-commit flow mutates only a detached copy and publishes through the table's commit member, so the mutation target verifies as the live object before any live-mutation or partial-mutation flag — and raw multi-product host output converts to owned handles at the continuation head so rollback releases through handle custody; receipt evidence reading host side-channels computes before any product mints so a throwing projection fails before custody exists, a mutation receipt derives from the landing-phase trace stamped where contact begins — never from static request classification — and a commit receipt stamps only after the commit's positive proof lands; every host-event observer builds its fact and invokes its sink inside one catch frame so a consumer fault never escapes the dispatcher, every callback seam in a folder rides one shared guarded-invocation owner centralizing error-sink dispatch and secondary-failure combination with callback failures retained as per-lease evidence — never reduced to unobservable side effects — and a best-effort secondary projection folds its refusal into the committed receipt's optional-error slot, never failing the primary; an open/closed staging cell makes closure the empty state of the same atomic cell so producer/closer racing is structurally impossible — queue state and lifecycle state fold into one closed union cell inspected and replaced atomically — never a flag beside the collection, separately synchronized cells, or a read-then-clear pair where one atomic exchange owns the drain; a key-to-role map at a join seam is allow-list and completeness obligation at once, unmatched declared keys failing with the missing set as evidence; a payload field one backend consumes natively maps to an equivalent construction on every other backend, never silently dropped; registration claims an atomic slot with a unique token, invokes the host only for the winning claim, and on host failure removes only its own token; index and identity admission validates against live table counts with uniqueness and required-result rules in one family, sentinels explicitly admitted; a completion receipt proves expected coverage, uniqueness, validity, and host completion together — count equality alone is not proof; a host product minted mid-pipeline rides an owned lease through every downstream stage, disposed on every failure path including completed-prefix release.
- A reviewer reports and demands, never edits. Every finding carries the file, the anchor, the defect, and the stronger form demanded — precise enough that a fixer agent lands the repair without re-deriving the analysis.
- Cross-surface coupling is standing duty: `docs/laws/topology.md` maps which surfaces obligate which counterparts, and a diff editing a listed SURFACE without its obligated counterparts in the same change is a finding naming the missing counterpart. `docs/laws/scars.md` and `docs/laws/patterns.md` laws are standing review duty.
- Suppressions and gate bypasses are the finding, never the mechanics the gates already own: a true positive is architecture pressure to fix the shape, a false positive is rule pressure to refine the rule, and a suppression directive is neither. Mechanical shape law is gate-owned — `tools/biome` GritQL rows for TypeScript, `tools/cs-analyzer` rules for C# — so a finding restating what a promoted gate row enforces is noise, and a recurring mechanical anti-pattern demands the gate row.
- Judge composition idiomatically: an interface earns its existence through dispatch or substitution, and generated or source-gen surfaces run at intended power — decorative abstraction is a finding.
- A fence member whose body is a placeholder comment (`=> /* ... */ ;` or an empty braced body carrying only prose) is an illusory implementation — reject it before deeper review, because the surrounding prose asserts capability no route materializes. A resource-release claim in prose or comments — bracket release, abort-on-every-exit, dispose-on-fault — binds to a visible call in the shown body, and a design upgrading auto-cleanup to durable resume deletes the release claim in the same edit.
- Sweep every fence for local compilability before content review — planning fences have no compiler, so these fictions survive fluent prose: a `ref struct` operand captured by a lambda or riding a generic carrier, an identifier neither declared in scope nor owned by the page, a value returned from a `void` member, a brace-only body where a return type demands a value, a type name written as an invocation, a generated-owner row whose positional argument count drifts from its declared constructor arity, a railed factory result — a `Result`- or rail-returning entry — bound into a bare-typed slot with no flatten, a payload passed outside the factory's declared admitted union, a bare `guard(...)` arm or `guard(...).Map(...)` where the rail projection must precede any combinator, a rail-valued lambda relying on implicit `Error`-to-`Validation` widening outside a target-typed position, a traversal or fold trait member spelled on a bare array or `FrozenSet` with no `toSeq(...)` lift, a one-parameter arm on a state-threaded `[Union]` `Switch` or a two-parameter arm on a state-threaded `[SmartEnum]` one — union arms take `(state, case)`, smart-enum arms the state alone — a `Switch` whose arms return sibling case types with no union-base cast anchoring the first arm (CS0411), a `[UseDelegateFromConstructor]` member carrying an authored body where the generator owns the implementation from the constructor-supplied delegate, and a derivation from a registry row — a fault-band offset, a parity slot, a wire key — whose registry declaration does not exist.
- A closed union whose every case carries one identically-typed field re-projected by a total switch is operand restatement — demand the shared value as the consuming entrypoint's argument with cases keeping only per-occurrence policy; two cases byte-identical in payload and behavior, differing only in name, collapse to one case whose discriminant derives from the op's trait row.
- Attack fold accumulators: a seed starting completeness flags true fabricates a computed receipt over an empty input set — demand an explicit empty-set rail or false-seeded flags, with one sibling fold railing empty while its neighbours do not as the tell; a fact stream folded into a name-keyed bag silently drops all but one entry under a repeated key — set-valued findings emit as one typed list value under one name; a subset test over a requirement row (`need <= available`) reads an empty requirement as vacuously satisfied, silently inverting a disabled row into always-enabled — demand the explicit empty-row short-circuit.
- A safety guarantee a shared rail claims — fence, lock, guarded predicate, tenant guard — is consumed structurally by the rail body from the case's data row; a per-case executable delegate whose body carries the guarantee makes the claim illusory and unreviewable.
- Prose signatures byte-match their fence declarations: an Entry/Auto row whose parameter list, rail, or return drifts from the fence is a wrong contract a downstream composer builds against, and a fence signature edit ripples its prose rows in the same diff.
- In a closed dispatch family folding one shared request shape, every column is consumed or loudly refused by every arm — an axis some arms read and others silently ignore converts an unrepresentable check into a silent pass. Trace every declared policy or vocabulary column to the executable arm its card claims: a column consumed only by a cache key, receipt, or log annotates rather than governs — demand the governing arm or the demotion. An arm that cannot honor a policy field refuses the combination loudly before provider work — a silently weakened or partially applied policy is the finding.

## [02]-[SEVERITY]

Grade across the full scale. High marks a correctness, contract, or architecture breach a consumer inherits; medium marks a weak form with a demanded stronger one; low marks residue worth draining. A review where every finding lands at one severity has averaged, not graded — and a pass that finds nothing after genuine attack states that as its verdict, never padded with manufactured findings.

## [03]-[DO_NOT_FLAG]

Deliberate house shapes that violate generic best practice — flagging them is a false positive:

- Refuted standing claims, banned verbatim at every severity — the named page read precedes any py3.15 syntax-validity or pinned-provider severity: "frozendict is not importable from builtins" (`from builtins import frozendict` is the legislated table spelling, `docs/stacks/python/language.md` [FROZENDICT_TABLE_SITE]); "start_soon returns None" (`start_soon -> TaskHandle[T]`, `handle.return_value` read after group close, `docs/stacks/python/concurrency.md` [CHILD_CARRIER]); "except A, B: is invalid syntax" (paren-less multi-exception is py3.14+ grammar and the ruff-format canonical form the repo gate emits).
- Aggressive API breaks with every call site updated in the same change: the sanctioned rename path.
- Dense single-expression bodies and heavy polymorphic dispatch: the bar, not obfuscation.
- Absent defensive guards inside domain logic: admission-once boundaries, not missing error handling.
- Sparse 1-2 line agent-facing comments: comment-law compliance, not missing documentation.
- A large file owning one full concern: sanctioned; file-size budgets do not exist here.
- Explicit match/dispatch arms over merged ternaries, and named intermediates on result rails: deliberate.
- Bracketed uppercase section dividers (`# --- [LABEL] ---`): source structure, not commented-out code.
- `TODO`/`FIXME` as source comment markers: sanctioned; the ban applies only to durable markdown.
- `[QUEUED]`/`[ACTIVE]`/`[BLOCKED]` task status and marked RESEARCH items: the planning corpus's closed vocabulary, never process-ledger or hedge findings.
- `lazy import`/`lazy from` at module scope: py3.15 corpus-floor spellings the Python doctrine legislates and its own fences spell — never invalid syntax or a misplaced import, at any severity.
- `tuple()` match patterns: class patterns matching any tuple instance — only the literal `()` is empty-only.
- Typed-field re-validation demands — `isfinite` on a typed int, arity re-checks on a typed frozen field: admission-once law makes the demand the defect.
- Manifest-admitted packages under a temporary distribution gate: a central-manifest row carrying a sanctioned gate comment and named follow-up is design-real — verification is the manifest admission, never wheel availability, and demanding a design page drop the package is the capability-gating defect.
- SHM release-in-finally, bare-ndarray-only span dispatch, DEGRADED-keeps-serving rolls, and pid custody by complement over owned sets: ruled worker-fabric contracts on their owning pages, never lifecycle bugs.
- Tagged-union faults consumed as values: no BaseException lineage exists, so a re-raise demand inverts the one-direction exception-to-fault law.
- Five-field Preview with its trailing band, and in-place delta bounds by shift-displaced occupancy: contract shapes the owning pages rule.
- `msgspec.UNSET`/`pydantic.experimental.MISSING` collapsed at the seam, and `expression.extra.result.catch(exception=X)` as the exception trap: the codec's own presence sentinels and the substrate's ruled adapter — demanding `effect.try_` as the exception adapter, or a domain absence axis on the wire sentinel, inverts the ruling. `expression.Map` requires a total key order, so an insertion-order-dependent derivation riding `frozendict` is the fix, never `Map`; an owned enum mirroring a provider vocabulary owes the full member roster — `EnumType(foreign_value)` raises on every dropped member.
- Strata diagrams omitting unsourced members, same-stratum edges, or foreign interior owners: template law resolves nodes by sourcing decision, same-stratum reads as prose, interiors home-side, islands as prose.
- Specs importing the private-by-design testkit through its exports-map or project-graph surface, and magic values in specs: sanctioned; a relative-path reach-around into kit internals is the finding.
- Rhino-rich host capture coexisting with host-neutral semantics: architecture, never duplication.
- C# `partial` struct/class/record with no visible body, and attribute walls of `OperatorsGeneration` knobs on a `[ValueObject]`: generator-completed owners and load-bearing algebra grants, never empty types or noise.
- `Option<TVo> = default` plus `IfNone` on an optional parameter: the CS1736/TTRESG047 fix and the one optional-parameter form on every rail surface — a ValueObject/SmartEnum default is uncompilable, the Option spelling is never a smell, and the nullable-defaulting-null parameter is the finding instead.
- `.As()` re-anchoring after trait combinators, and `Try.lift(...).Run().MapFail(...).Bind(static r => r)` self-flatten: mandatory LanguageExt mechanics — dropping either breaks typing or discards the error; a first-arm cast to the union base where `Switch` arms return sibling case types is the CS0411 common-type anchor, never a redundant cast, and `toSeq(...)` lifting a bare array or `FrozenSet` before `TraverseM`/`Traverse`/`Fold` is the required traversable instance, never ceremony.
- Pinned LanguageExt semantics carry inverse defects: `Schedule` union (`|`) runs to the longer operand — a finite curve unioned onto an infinite one claimed as a bound is the finding, intersect (`&`) or a cumulative-delay gate the fix; retry wraps each attempt fail-only while repeat wraps every iteration unconditionally, so a repeated computation acquiring and returning a live resource is the finding; `Fin<T>` failures all equal each other and hash to one constant — a set or dictionary keyed on `Fin` coalesces failures, and the projected scalar discriminant is the demanded key; `TraverseM`'s trait-default serializes independent effects under a monadic name.
- GH2 and RhinoCommon host truths, member spellings included: an apparent typo in a decompile-verified host member (`DisplayPipelineAttributes.ShowSilhouttes`, `NamedParametersEventArgs.TryGetUnmanged`) is the shipped spelling — a misspelling flag or corrected-spelling demand breaks the binding and is void at every severity; `ObjectList.AllWires` includes dangling entries by design (consumer filters), `Canvas.ScheduleRedraw` with the before-background paint phase is the canonical motion tick, and `KeyedValues.Set` is typed-overload-only — never library bugs or missing generics; display-mode descriptors are owned copies — `GetDisplayMode` returns a copy and registration changes publish only through `UpdateDisplayMode`/`AddDisplayMode`, so a mutates-live-registration claim against a copy-then-publish flow is void.
- `yield*` on a bare `Option`/`Either` inside `Effect.gen`, and inline type specifiers on one `import` statement under `verbatimModuleSyntax`: Effect subtype and compiler requirements, never missing lifts or style drift.
- `Redacted` printing `<redacted>` on every channel with `unsafeWipe` terminal, compared through `Redacted.getEquivalence`: safety by construction, never missing discipline; `Arbitrary.make`/`Pretty.make` throw `Missing annotation` over a bare `Schema.declare` while `Schema.equivalence` silently falls back to reference identity — the silent fallback is the audit line, so a foreign owner admits with its annotation set complete or not at all.
- `LanguageExt.HashSet` and its persistent kin read as mutable BCL collections: the carrier is immutable and persistent — update operations return values, `IsEmpty`/`Exists`/folds are its surface — so a mutation-semantics or shared-state claim against it is false, and namespace qualification is the standing recurrence guard.
- `Validation`-before-`Traverse` accumulation with `ToFin` after the traversal: applicative traversal evaluates the complete sequence and accumulates every failure — a first-failure short-circuit claim is false.
- Host reads inside a `Fin` catch lambda: `Bind`/`Map`/`TraverseM` evaluate strictly there, so the surrounding catch frame already captures them — an escape-the-rail claim is false, and the demanded form pulls every seam host read, census and count reads included, inside that frame.
- Interior null-admission demands under nullable-enable with warnings-as-errors: non-nullable members, `readonly record struct` parameters, and union-case payloads are checked contracts, union positional cases construct only over already-admitted payloads, and seam guards ride the host entry weave — a runtime null re-fold demand re-spells the compile contract. Host geometry value-struct payloads — `Line`, `Arc`, `Circle`, `Plane`, `Sphere`, `Cylinder`, and kin — spell no null by construction; only reference-bearing `Curve`, `Surface`, `Mesh`, and `Brep` admit null at host seams, so a null-guard demand on a struct payload is void.
- Default-ghost storage seams on `readonly partial struct` value objects: field zero-init and `default` mint admission-bypassing ghosts, so the one outer seam reading the key member is mandated — a redundant-check removal demand against it is the false positive.
- Full-roster restore brackets: folding the complete facet roster is the page's deterministic-scope contract and the subset fold leaks ambient state — never a partial-write defect.
- Same-shape sibling address unions on genuinely different case sets: the distinct roster is the collapse boundary — a dedup demand across it is false.
- Wire-shape and carrier-arity claims settled by the owning constructor: a normalizing constructor folding array and `Seq<T>` into one payload while publishing the array slot shape, and an anchor-row carrier keeping one outer row per query anchor with its complete set within, are contract shapes — read the owner before flagging.
- Generated-owner admission order: a generated `[SmartEnum]` admits no invalid instance by construction — a membership guard against undeclared enum items is structurally void — and generator-owned absence guards precede `ValidateFactoryArguments`, so an interior normalization hook owes no null-or-absence re-check.
- `using var` on a scoped disposable boundary carrier, and `var` bindings inside pattern arms — list, property, positional, and slice: doctrine-prescribed spellings, so restructure or explicit-type demands against them are style noise at every severity.
- Upward stratum delegation demands: the owning ARCHITECTURE strata table prohibits a lower stratum referencing a higher one, so a finding demanding direct upward delegation is refuted by the table — the injected-seam form, the higher stratum supplying its capability through a parameter, is the legal shape.
- Lease-ownership demands settle at the host type's decompile-proven `IDisposable` lineage — custody lands as an owned lease through total union dispatch, so a per-site dispose demand the lease rail already owns, or a custody claim contradicting the lineage, is false.
