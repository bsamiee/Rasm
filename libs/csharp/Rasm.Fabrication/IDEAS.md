# [FABRICATION_IDEAS]

The forward concept pool for `Rasm.Fabrication`. Each idea is a card — a bracketed slug leader plus a few bullets stating the capability, what it unlocks, and the gap or modern technique it draws on. An idea drives one or more `TASKLOG.md` tasks; a finished or dropped idea moves to `[2]-[CLOSED]` with a one-line disposition.

[PROCESS_MACHINE_AXIS]:
- The one missing top-level fabrication discriminant: a `Process` `[SmartEnum<string>]` (`mill`/`turn`/`route`/`laser`/`plasma`/`waterjet`/`additive`/`oxyfuel`/`edm-wire`) and a `Machine` `[SmartEnum<string>]` (`mill-3axis`/`mill-5axis`/`router-gantry`/`lathe-2axis`/`lathe-millturn`/`laser-flatbed`/`plasma-table`/`waterjet-5axis`/`fff-cartesian`/`fff-delta`) axis pair, each carrying its kinematic class, its removal modality (subtractive/thermal/additive/abrasive), its motion family, and its post dialect column. - Frontier re-seed (the open pool was emptied of its real domain depth at I-pass close): the recovered process/machine families are the folder's spine. Draws on the universal modality-completeness law (every axis that varies by where code runs carries the full row set) applied to fabrication's process modality — the removal physics, the motion kinematics, and the controller dialect are three columns on one `Process`/`Machine` axis, never three baked milling assumptions.
