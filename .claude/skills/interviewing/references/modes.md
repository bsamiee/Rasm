# [MODES]

Each mode is the operating loop specialized to one extraction; the card carries entry, grounding scope, named stages, and what lands. Stages chain by input: every stage names the prior stage's product as its declared input and carries the exit test that gates the next, so a card reads as a conveyor from grounding to landing. A stage whose input cannot reach its exit diverges by the branch the card names, never an unbounded retry of the same stage. Ephemeral working forms are declared inline; durable kinds carry their contracts in the skill entry's kinds table and compose as their html-studio types.

## [01]-[INTERVIEW]

- Enter: Requirements ambiguous, contradictory, or living in the user's head; a brief, spec, or roadmap about to be authored from a thin prompt.
- Ground: The axes the request touches, plus the user's starting position — what they explored, tried, and rejected; the product is the confrontation set of verified anchors.
- Start: Establish the user's position from the confrontation set, drawing out what they built, tried, and already rejected (exit: the dump repeats known facts and surfaces no new constraint, option, or position).
- Frame: Route every unknown the dump named to its bucket, running blindspot or brainstorm first where their signals dominate (exit: each bucket with signal is covered and the interview retains only residual known unknowns).
- Thread: Per residual learning target, plan the branch tree — the load-bearing question anchored in the confrontation set, and for each anticipated answer the follow-on it opens down to the terminal ruling (exit: three targets each carry a tree whose root question is undetermined, consequential, and ranked).
- Round: Advance every live thread one stratum, batching independent questions and routing answers to their branches; re-plan a tree when an answer falls off it, and retire the thread to Frame when a verified fact kills the root's premise. Three or more independent costed questions serve as an html-studio decision surface; receipt answers face the standard challenge (exit: every thread seals in a ruling or a tracked open item; two rulingless rounds seal a thread as open).
- Lands: The ruling record as working form; each settled ruling a future session consults as law graduates to a `decision-record`, and roadmap-altitude rulings compose a `roadmap-brief`.
- Terminate: Every ruling marked, no conflict open, the fold-back prompt emitted.

## [02]-[BLINDSPOT_PASS]

- Enter: Unfamiliar territory, a plan or corpus section about to seal, an explicit "what am I missing", or the unknown-unknowns bucket dominating the frame.
- Ground: The full axis catalog over the target — this mode is the grounding pass weaponized; a target beyond one reading budget partitions on both dimensions of the scaling law, subtree legs carrying the local axes over one folder subtree and seam-spine legs carrying the cross-cutting axes end to end, because a cross-cutting axis confined to subtree legs reports clean while the seam hides the finding.
- Probe: Run each selected axis's probe over the target and collect candidate findings (exit: every touched axis probed, clean axes recorded clean).
- Verify: Re-open each candidate's anchor on disk and drop every one the corpus does not carry (exit: zero unverified survivors).
- Rank: Fold the survivors that overlapping legs report as one finding into a single entry carrying every leg's anchor, order the result by blast radius, and challenge the target's own settled lines with the ASSUMPTIONS_CHECK and PROSPECTIVE_HINDSIGHT protocols, giving each its anchor, consequence, and fold-back (exit: every finding carries anchor, consequence, and fold-back, and no finding is reported twice).
- Lands: A `blindspot-ledger`; zero findings is a legal result stated plainly with the probed axes listed. Findings from the CAPABILITY_WEAKNESS and TOUCH_POINT axes that state standing capability facts land as `capability-entry` rows beside the ledger. A ledger triaged live serves as an html-studio editor whose `[FOLDED]`/`[REFUSED]` status flips return as a receipt; the instance stays the truth the editor projects.
- Terminate: The ledger seals when a cold read needs no transcript; the fold-back prompt folds the findings into the originating task.

## [03]-[BRAINSTORM]

- Enter: A named problem with multiple viable interventions and no committed direction; the unknown-knowns bucket dominating — the user will recognize the right direction only on sight.
- Ground: Constraints and kill conditions only — the real limits any direction must survive. An existing solution, sibling design, or prior direction met while grounding is quarantined as a seed and admitted only at expand, because a seed seen before generation narrows the category search.
- Generate: From the constraint frame alone, generate a free pool across the tiers — patch, structural, rebuild — before any seed or prior-art exposure (exit: two tiers carry distinct theses).
- Expand: Force category jumps on the free pool — mechanism, goal, inversion, failure, scale, horizon, consumer — folding the quarantined seeds in only now (exit: no two directions share a thesis).
- Attack: Attack each direction with disconfirmation; the wargame variant scores on weights declared before any direction is scored; a direction surviving no disconfirmation exits rejected with its kill anchor, and a kill felling every direction re-grounds the fan against the indicted constraint (exit: a surviving direction leads or the binding constraint is re-grounded; a scored choice carries one row per direction).
- Lands: A `direction-set` with the leading direction and the flip triggers that overturn it; the rendered comparison page is html-studio ground, served through the return channel so the user's pick, scores, and steal-selections come back as a submission receipt.
- Terminate: The user reacts to rendered directions — receipt or spoken — the ruling is recorded, rejected directions keep their reopen conditions.

## [04]-[QUIZ]

- Enter: Work about to merge or seal with comprehension of the change unproven.
- Ground: The diff's least-visited corners, the decisions that almost went the other way, the failure modes — never the summary.
- Items: From the grounded corners, author application shapes answerable only through the changed causal model — predict the output of an input not in the diff, name what breaks when a changed predicate inverts, transfer the invariant to an adjacent boundary, map a symptom back to its mechanism, select the repair that preserves the invariant (exit: every item maps to a merge-critical invariant and the section that teaches it).
- Score: Each item's options homogeneous with three effective choices, every wrong answer carrying one diagnosable misconception, and a selected answer carrying a one-sentence causal justification that must agree with it (exit: scored, no answer-justification mismatch).
- Gate: Criterion, never percentage — every merge-critical invariant correct; a miss re-teaches from the mapped section and retests the same invariant through a fresh isomorphic scenario, never the revealed item, and a second miss on that invariant is a design gap rather than a reader gap — it stops the retest and hands the merge to Terminate (exit: every critical invariant correct on renewed retrieval, or its second miss ruled a design gap and routed).
- Lands: The scored gate, ephemeral; the quiz page runs served through the html-studio return channel so selections and justifications return as one receipt the gate scores. A wrong answer that exposed a real design gap graduates as a `blindspot-ledger` finding.
- Terminate: The criterion gate clears or an explicit decision holds the merge.

## [05]-[BUY_IN]

- Enter: A decision needing acceptance from a reviewer or stakeholder outside the loop.
- Ground: The evidence in the work — demo, spec, ruling record, implementation notes — and the objection surface of each audience.
- Lead: Open the packet with the inspectable artifact from the grounded evidence — demo, trace, before-and-after run — chosen so it exposes the adoption attributes: advantage over the current way, workflow fit, bounded trial, observable result (exit: the packet opens with evidence, not history).
- Objections: Against the opened packet, state each audience's strongest objection in its own terms and refute it with the diagnostic signal proving the refutation, one lane per opposed audience — an objection acknowledged without refutation reads worse than silence, and one the work cannot refute is never force-answered but stands as an open conflict, its evidence demand named as the ask (exit: every objection carries its refutation and signal, or an open conflict naming its evidence demand).
- Asks: Name the asks the refuted packet earns and the sign-off list that clears them (exit: every ask has an owner).
- Lands: The packet, ephemeral, rendered through the html-studio buy-in type and served so sign-offs and objections return as one receipt per audience; the accepted decision graduates to a `decision-record` and the packet dies.
- Terminate: Sign-off recorded or the objection that blocked it enters the ruling record as an open conflict.

## [06]-[TEACH_ME]

- Enter: The user cannot evaluate options or phrase intent — vague vocabulary, gesture-level requests, a domain where the user cannot yet judge what good looks like.
- Ground: The minimal concept set the pending decisions require, drawn from the corpus and the domain — never the full domain tour.
- Contrast: From the minimal concept set, open with contrasting cases — two to four aligned examples differing on the dimensions the pending decision turns on — and ask what differs before naming anything; visually or interactively discriminated dimensions render through the html-studio explainer or contact-sheet type, where a felt slider teaches what a definition cannot (exit: the user names real differences unprompted).
- Name: Give the terms and discriminating dimensions the contrast exposed, prompting explicit comparison so the user aligns the cases rather than studying them separately (exit: the user restates their intent in the new terms).
- Check: Prove evaluation ability on a fresh pair, fading the teaching as competence appears — guidance past competence is load; a ranking still wrong after a re-scoped contrast round means the decision turns on no distinction learnable in-session and routes to the interviewer as a defaulted ruling with its assumption marked, never a third contrast (exit: the ranking is defended in the taught terms and the pending decision is phrasable, or the decision is defaulted with its assumption marked).
- Lands: The vocabulary map — vague term, technical term, the decision it unlocks — ephemeral; it binds the follow-on mode the way the ruling record binds authoring.
- Terminate: The user's restated request needs no translation.

## [07]-[NOTES]

- Enter: Implementation in flight surfaces a fact the plan did not carry — a code-revealed contradiction of a plan claim, a missing owner, an edge no ruling faced — and the discovery is about to vanish into scrollback.
- Ground: The plan claim the discovery strikes and the ruling-record entry it premises on; the discovery anchors to the code fact that revealed it, never to the builder's impression of it.
- Log: Record each deviation as it lands under the fixed schema — plan claim, code-revealed fact, conservative choice taken, revisit condition — while the build keeps moving; the conservative choice is the reversible default preserving the most downstream options where judgment is absent, and a deviation logged without its revisit condition converts judgment debt into silent drift (exit: every deviation carries all four fields and no build step blocked waiting on a ruling).
- Triage: Classify each deviation local — absorbed by the conservative choice, folded forward as a plan correction — or ruling-striking — it invalidates a standing entry in the ruling record or the plan's premise set (exit: every deviation carries exactly one class and every ruling-striking one names the entry it strikes).
- Lands: The deviation ledger, ephemeral. A ruling-striking deviation graduates: the overturned record entry is edited in place with its superseding ruling, a standing discovery lands as a `decision-record` or a `blindspot-ledger` finding, and the fold-back bullets rewrite the next plan or prompt from the surprises.
- Terminate: The ledger empties by graduation or fold-back; a deviation surviving only in scrollback is the failure this mode exists to prevent.
