# [REWRITING]

Rebuilding an existing document is harder than writing a fresh one: the source's frame is the primary contaminant, and an agent that reads before arming itself inherits the frame — re-wording the poison, preserving the anchors, and calling it a fix. This procedure binds every pass that touches an existing durable doc.

## [01]-[CONTAMINATION]

- [READING_IS_ANCHORING]: A source's section set, emphasis, enumerations, and justifications become the rewrite's defaults unless each is re-derived; what the source treats as law reads as law, including its defects.
- [LOCAL_CONTEXT_LIES]: A page drifted from corpus law is internally consistent and looks finished judged against itself; the rewrite aligns to corpus law and the sibling exemplar, never to the page's own precedent.
- [POSTURE_TRANSFERS]: A source's deference rides along with its facts — maturity labels, ecosystem defaults, risk framing, endorsed paths; payload extraction keeps the fact and flips the authority to the corpus ruling, or the rewrite re-ships the upstream's register as law.
- [SOFTENING_IS_INHERITANCE]: Trimming a litany to three corpses, shortening a frozen chant, keeping a growth register in reserve — each preserves the frame at lower volume. A defect class is removed by reframing the passage, never by dieting it.

## [02]-[PROCEDURE]

1. [ARM] — Load the law before the target: the defect catalog, the register, the structure law, and the sibling exemplar of the target's file kind. Read the target as evidence, never as guidance.
2. [EXTRACT] — Keep exactly three things from the source: facts that fail the regeneration test, mechanism that has an owner here or lower, and open questions that become tracked items. Everything else is frame.
3. [CLASSIFY] — Every suspect passage gets a defect class before any fix; a passage that resists classification is either legal register or a new class worth naming, and an unclassified suspect never survives by default.
4. [REBUILD] — Author the new document from the exemplar's shape and the extracted payload; the source is consulted for payload completeness, never for structure, order, emphasis, or wording.
5. [DEMOTE] — Mechanism found above its altitude moves to its owner before the higher copy dies; capability found gated or defended lands as present-tense law; open work moves to the card owner. Only then is the defective prose deleted.
6. [RECONCILE] — A fact moved or an owner renamed touches its twins: the counterpart seam, the pointing consumer, the registry row. Both ends land in the same pass; a reported-not-fixed ripple is an unfinished rewrite.

## [03]-[WORKED_REFRAME]

One passage through the whole procedure — classified `CAPABILITY_GATE` + `PROCESS_LEDGER` + `SET_IN_STONE`, payload extracted, reframed at the owner:

```markdown rejected
DECISION [V7]: the export lane is SEALED at three formats — LANDED, never re-opened. A fourth format row stays in the growth register and re-opens only when a consumer names it; until then the register is the deferred-capability record of note.
```

```markdown accepted
Export lands through one format vocabulary: a format is one row carrying its encoder, media type, and policy defaults, and the lane folds any admitted row without a consumer edit.
```

A correct rewrite is shorter, carries more capability, and leaves nothing to re-open: the seal, the tag, the register, and the consumer condition all died with the frame.

## [04]-[TABLE_REPAIR]

A broken table is rebuilt through the structural moves, never flattened: teardown into mega-prose lines or a bare unlabeled list destroys the structure the fix was meant to save, and is the same hamfisted inheritance as softening. Each move is the table law's, composed as given; this procedure owns only the order:

1. [ELIGIBILITY_FIRST] — Test the table against the eligibility triple before touching cells; a failed test converts the whole container to a record set, indexed list, or cards, and a passed test repairs in place.
2. [MOVES] — Run the refactor moves in place; the cell keeps its atomic value and the index column re-numbers.
3. [DEMOTE_LAST] — A row family that resists every move leaves table form as an indexed list or record set with its structure intact — index, label, and payload survive the conversion.

## [05]-[LIST_REPAIR]

A mega bullet is a compressed section wearing a hyphen: a law, its mechanism, two consequences, an exception, and an example fused into one entry. Repair classifies the fragments and routes each to its container — splitting the sentence run into sibling bullets without classification just shreds the compression.

1. [CLASSIFY] — Dissect the entry into fragments: the law, mechanism, consequence, exception, example, duplicate. Only the law is the bullet's decision; everything else is cargo.
2. [KEEP_THE_LAW] — A bullet keeps the law in one to two sentences with its single load-bearing reason.
3. [ROUTE_THE_CARGO] — Mechanism moves to the owning fence or page; consequences that govern siblings move to the section lead; a genuine exception becomes a contract qualifier on the law or its own entry; examples become fences beside the rule; duplicates die.
4. [PROMOTE] — An entry whose fragments are all load-bearing at this altitude is not a bullet: promote it to a GroupedRecord card with earned fields, a labeled block, or a subsection.
5. [PSEUDO_LIST] — Entries answering different questions are separate containers, and numbered items that are not a sequence become bullets or prose; a narrative that reads continuously de-lists into a paragraph.

## [06]-[EXIT_TESTS]

A rewrite is done only when it survives every test; failing any one means the frame leaked through.

- [COLD_READ]: A fresh agent reading only the result cannot reconstruct the source's structure, emphasis, or history — no seam shows where the old frame was.
- [REGENERATION]: Every surviving sentence is law an agent cannot regenerate from disk and stated invariants; everything regenerable is fenced or gone.
- [REGISTER]: Every law sentence has an owning subject and owning verb; no sentence's subject is a forbidden alternative, a consumer count, a process artifact, or the document itself, and no sentence freezes, seals, or legislates a count against rebuild.
- [VOLUME]: A finished rewrite carries fewer lines than the source with zero payload lost — the demotion count proves the payload moved. A rewrite that grew re-imported frame; a rewrite that shrank by dropping mechanism destroyed capability.

## [07]-[PERSISTENCE]

- [AGREEMENT_IS_THE_TRAP]: A source's framing matching the first-draft instinct is the strongest anchor — that match is the signal to re-derive, not to proceed; shared instinct is how the same defect re-enters twice.
- [AUTHORITY_LEAKAGE]: A passage carrying a seal, a ruling tag, or a never-re-open clause is prose, not law — the rebuild mandate outranks a page's self-declared immutability. True freezes live only outside the document: declared wire contracts at their codec owner and the corpus standards themselves.
- [SUNK_COST_PROSE]: Length, density, and citation apparatus signal effort, not correctness; a forty-line justification block extracts to one policy row more often than to forty lines of law.
- [CONFLICT_ROUTING]: Corpus law wins over the page without negotiation; a conflict between two corpus laws surfaces to the standards owner and is never resolved silently inside one page.
